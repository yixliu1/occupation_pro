<h1>Occupation Name: Chief Executive or Managing Director</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 111111</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: AIM</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Chief Executive or Managing Director determines, formulates and reviews the general policy program and the overall direction of an organisation within the framework established by a board of directors or similar governing body.</p>
<p>Chief Executive or Managing Director may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>determining objectives, strategies, policies and programs for organisations</li>
<li>providing overall direction and management to organisations</li>
<li>authorising material, human and financial resources to implement organisational policies and programs</li>
<li>monitoring and evaluating performance of organisations against organisational objectives and strategies</li>
<li>consulting with senior subordinate staff and reviewing recommendations and reports</li>
<li>preparing, or arranging for the preparation of, reports, budgets and forecasts and presenting them to governing bodies</li>
<li>representing the organisation at official occasions, in negotiations, at conventions, seminars, public hearings and forums, and liaising between areas of responsibility</li>
<li>selecting, or approving the selection of senior staff</li>
<li>ensuring the organisation complies with company laws and other relevant legislation</li>
