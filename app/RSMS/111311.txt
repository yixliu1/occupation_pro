<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Local Government Legislator represents the interests of people in a constituency as their elected member of a local government authority.</p>
<p>Local Government Legislator may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>developing national, state, territory or local government policy, and formulating, amending and repealing legislation and by-laws</li>
<li>helping constituents with a range of problems particularly with regard to matters concerning government and local agencies</li>
<li>investigating matters of concern to the public and to particular persons and groups</li>
<li>introducing proposals for government action and representing public and electoral interests</li>
<li>issuing policy directions to government departments and exercising control over local government authorities</li>
<li>attending community functions and meetings of local groups to provide service to the community, gauge public opinion and provide information on government plans</li>
<li>may serve as a member on parliamentary committees and inquiries</li>
<li>may present petitions on behalf of concerned groups</li>
