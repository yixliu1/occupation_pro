<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Member of Parliament represents the interests of people in a constituency as their elected member to national, state or territory parliament.</p>
<p>Parliamentarians may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>present issues for debate and discussion in parliament</li>
<li>propose and debate new legislation and changes to existing legislation</li>
<li>develop policy that best serves the interests of the public and the electorate</li>
<li>investigate matters of concern to the public or particular interest groups</li>
<li>present petitions on behalf of concerned interest groups</li>
<li>serve on parliamentary committees or enquiries</li>
<li>manage an office in their home electorate and in the house of parliament.</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation is on the Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme Occupation List (RSMS ROL) , you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclass:</p>
<li>Regional Sponsor Migration Scheme (subclass 187)</li>
