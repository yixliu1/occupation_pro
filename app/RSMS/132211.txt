<h1>Occupation Name: Finance Manager</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 132211</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: CAANZ/CPAA/IPA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Finance Managers plan, organise, direct, control and coordinate the financial and accounting activities within organisations.</p>
<p>Finance Manager may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>determining, implementing, monitoring, reviewing and evaluating budgetary and accounting strategies, policies and plans in consultation with other Managers</li>
<li>providing financial information and interpreting the implications for business performance and funding needs</li>
<li>coordinating the development, implementation and monitoring of accounting systems</li>
<li>directing the preparation of financial reports that summarise and forecast the organisation's financial position such as income statements, balance sheets and analyses of future earnings and income</li>
<li>assessing capital finance proposals and the financial status of operational projects</li>
<li>advising on investment strategies, sources of funds and the distribution of earnings</li>
<li>delivering long range profit forecasts, budgeting and financial reporting</li>
<li>ensuring compliance with financial legislation and standards</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation belongs to Short-term Skilled Occupation List (STSOL) . STSOL consists of the list of the occupations which are high in demand in Australia. If you want to apply for Australia sponsored Permanent Residency (subclass 190) your occupation must be listed in the relevant STSOL list and you must have an invitation/sponsorship from the state government. This list is also a part of the Australia Point system. If eligible, you can live and work in Australia for an indefinite period of time.</p>
<p>If your occupation is on STSOL, you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclasses:</p>
<li>Skilled Nominated visa (subclass 190)</li>
<li>Training visa (subclass 407)</li>
<li>Skilled Work Regional (Provisional) visa (subclass 491) - State or Territory nominated</li>
<li>Temporary Skill Shortage visa (subclass 482) - Short-term stream</li>
<li>Skilled Employer Sponsored Regional (Provisional) visa (Subclass 494)</li>
