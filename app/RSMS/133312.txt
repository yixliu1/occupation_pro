<h1>Occupation Name: Wholesaler</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 133312</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: VETASSESS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Wholesaler plans, organises, directs, controls and coordinates the operations of a wholesale trading establishment.</p>
<p>Wholesaler may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>identifying local and overseas business opportunities</li>
<li>developing and implementing business plans, and marketing, operating, human resource, pricing and credit policies and procedures</li>
<li>determining the mix of products and services to be provided and negotiating conditions of trade</li>
<li>liaising with local and overseas suppliers and distributors about orders and products</li>
<li>researching regulatory and statutory requirements affecting the importing, exporting, wholesaling and distribution of goods</li>
<li>monitoring business performance and preparing estimates, financial statements and reports of operations</li>
<li>appointing agents and distributors</li>
<li>arranging the shipping of goods into and out of the country</li>
<li>overseeing the display and sale of merchandise and preparation of product information for customer service staff and customers</li>
<li>implementing after-sales service procedures</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation is on the Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme Occupation List (RSMS ROL) , you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclass:</p>
<li>Regional Sponsor Migration Scheme (subclass 187)</li>
<li>Skilled Employer Sponsored Regional (Provisional) visa (Subclass 494)</li>
<h2>Requirements for Skills Assessment</h2>
<p>Wholesaler is classified as Group B occupations by Vetassess. The criteria for skills assessment of Group B occupations are the following :</p>
<li>Qualification(s) assessed at AQF Bachelor degree or higher degree with a highly relevant field of study and at least one year of post-qualification highly relevant employment in the last five years; </li>
<li>Qualification(s) assessed at AQF Bachelor degree or higher degree with an additional qualification at least Australian Qualifications Framework (AQF) Diploma level in a highly relevant field of study and at least two years of post qualification highly relevant employment in the last five years; </li>
<li>Qualification(s) assessed at AQF Bachelor degree or higher degree without a highly relevant field of study and at least three years of post-qualification highly relevant employment in the last five years;</li>
<p>According to Vetassess, if you have pre-qualification employment and if all of your employment is before your qualification assessed at the AQF bachelor degree level , you are required to have five years of highly relevant employment in addition to at least one year of highly relevant employment at an appropriate skill level completed in the last five years.</p>
