<h1>Occupation Name: Supply and Distribution Manager</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 133611</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: AIM</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Supply and Distribution Manager plans, organises, directs, controls and coordinates the supply, storage and distribution of goods produced by an organisation.</p>
<p>Supply and Distribution Manager may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>determining, implementing and monitoring purchasing, storage and distribution strategies, policies and plans</li>
<li>preparing and implementing plans to maintain required stock levels at minimum cost</li>
<li>negotiating contracts with suppliers to meet quality, cost and delivery requirements</li>
<li>monitoring and reviewing storage and inventory systems to meet supply requirements and control stock levels</li>
<li>operating recording systems to track all movements of supplies and finished goods, and ensuring re-ordering and re-stocking at optimal times</li>
<li>liaising with other departments and customers concerning requirements for outward goods and associated forwarding transportation</li>
<li>overseeing the recording of purchase, storage and distribution transactions</li>
<li>directing staff activities and monitoring their performance</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation belongs to Short-term Skilled Occupation List (STSOL) . STSOL consists of the list of the occupations which are high in demand in Australia. <li>Temporary Skill Shortage visa (subclass 482) - Short-term stream</li>
<li>Skilled Employer Sponsored Regional (Provisional) visa (Subclass 494)</li>
