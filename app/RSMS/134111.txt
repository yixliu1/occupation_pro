<h1>Occupation Name: Child Care Centre Manager</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 134111</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: ACECQA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Child Care Centre Manager plans, organises, directs, controls and coordinates the activities of a childcare centre or service including physical and human resources.</p>
<p>Child Care Centre Manager may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>developing and implementing programs to enhance the physical, social, emotional and intellectual development of young children</li>
<li>providing care for children in before-school, after-school, day, and vacation care centres</li>
<li>directing and supervising Child Carers in providing care and supervision for young children</li>
<li>ensuring the centre is a safe area for children, staff and visitors</li>
<li>complying with relevant government requirements and standards</li>
<li>liaising with parents</li>
<li>maintaining records and accounts for the centre</li>
<li>recruiting staff and coordinating professional development</li>
