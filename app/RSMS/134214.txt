<h1>Occupation Name: Welfare Centre Manager</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 134214</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: ACWA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Welfare Centre Manager plans, organises, directs, controls and coordinates the professional and administrative aspects of health and welfare programs and services.</p>
<p>They may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>providing overall direction and management for the service, facility, organisation or centre</li>
<li>developing, implementing and monitoring procedures, policies and standards for medical, nursing, allied health and administrative staff</li>
<li>coordinating and administering health and welfare programs and clinical services</li>
<li>monitoring and evaluating resources devoted to health, welfare, recreation, housing, employment, training and other community facilities and centres</li>
<li>controlling administrative operations such as budget planning, report preparation, expenditure on supplies, equipment and services</li>
<li>liaising with other health and welfare providers, boards and funding bodies to discuss areas of health and welfare service cooperation and coordination</li>
<li>advising government bodies about measures to improve health and welfare services and facilities</li>
<li>representing the organisation in negotiations, and at conventions, seminars, public hearings and forums</li>
<li>controlling selection, training and supervision of staff</li>
