<h1>Occupation Name: School Principal</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 134311</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: VETASSESS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>School Principal plans, organises, directs, controls and coordinates the educational and administrative aspects of primary, middle or intermediate and secondary schools including physical and human resources. Registration or licensing is required.</p>
<p>They may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>determining educational programs based on curricula set by educational authorities</li>
<li>implementing systems and procedures to monitor school performance and student enrolments</li>
<li>directing administrative and clerical activities concerning student admissions and educational services</li>
<li>preparing school budgets and maintaining budgetary controls</li>
<li>promoting the educational program, and representing schools in the wider community</li>
<li>supervising the maintenance of school facilities</li>
<li>enforcing a disciplinary code to create a safe and conducive environment for students and teachers</li>
<li>organising and implementing methods of raising additional funds in conjunction with parent groups</li>
<li>controlling selection, training and supervision of staff</li>
<li>may teach students</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation belongs to Short-term Skilled Occupation List (STSOL) . STSOL consists of the list of the occupations which are high in demand in Australia. If you want to apply for Australia sponsored Permanent Residency (subclass 190) your occupation must be listed in the relevant STSOL list and you must have an invitation/sponsorship from the state government. This list is also a part of the Australia Point system. If eligible, you can live and work in Australia for an indefinite period of time.</p>
<p>If your occupation is on STSOL, you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclasses:</p>
<li>Skilled Nominated visa (subclass 190)</li>
<li>Training visa (subclass 407)</li>
<li>Skilled Work Regional (Provisional) visa (subclass 491) - State or Territory nominated</li>
<li>Temporary Skill Shortage visa (subclass 482) - Short-term stream</li>
<li>Skilled Employer Sponsored Regional (Provisional) visa (Subclass 494)</li>
<h2>Requirements for Skills Assessment</h2>
<p>School Principal is classified as Group A occupations by Vetassess. The criteria for skills assessment of Group A occupations is to hold qualification(s) assessed at Australian Bachelor degree or higher degree with a highly relevant field of study and at least one year of post-qualification highly relevant employment in the last five years.</p>
