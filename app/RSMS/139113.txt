<h1>Occupation Name: Commissioned Police Officer</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 139113</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: VETASSESS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Commissioned Police Officer provides high level management to support the running of a geographical or operational section of a police service.</p>
<p>Commissioned Police Officer may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>establishing administrative and operational procedures by taking account of the organisation's operating environment</li>
<li>making policy decisions and accepting responsibility for operations, performance of staff, achievement of targets and adherence to budgets, standards and procedures</li>
<li>establishing lines of control and delegating responsibilities to subordinate staff</li>
<li>representing the organisation in dealings with other organisations and the public</li>
<li>controlling the collection and interpretation of management information to monitor performance</li>
<li>controlling the use of, and accounting for, the assets and facilities of the organisation</li>
<li>preparing budgets and other management plans</li>
<li>preparing reports, authorising the release of information, and handling public relations activities</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>

<li>Regional Sponsor Migration Scheme (subclass 187)</li>
