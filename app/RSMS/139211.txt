<h1>Occupation Name: Senior Non-commissioned Defence Force Member</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 139211</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: VETASSESS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Senior Non-Commissioned Defence Force Member implement and enforce directives of commissioned officers of the Australian and New Zealand Defence Forces.</p>
<p>Senior Non-commissioned Defence Force Member may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>supervising a small group of subordinates</li>
<li>maintaining discipline of subordinates</li>
<li>monitoring the morale, welfare and behaviour of subordinates, and advising commanding officers accordingly</li>
<li>instructing subordinates in dress, deportment, demeanour, behaviour, drill and devotion to duty to maintain military standards</li>
<li>conducting military drills and inspections</li>
<li>teaching military traditions and customs</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation is on the Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme Occupation List (RSMS ROL) , you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclass:</p>
<li>Regional Sponsor Migration Scheme (subclass 187)</li>
