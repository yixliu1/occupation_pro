<h1>Occupation Name: Arts Administrator or Manager</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 139911</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: VETASSESS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Arts Administrator or Manager plans, organises, directs, controls, coordinates and promotes artistic and cultural policies, programs, projects and services.</p>
<p>Arts administrators may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>seek sponsorship and funding from federal, state, territory and local governments as well as community and industry organisations</li>
<li>prepare and submit funding applications</li>
<li>negotiate with artists and performers regarding contracts</li>
<li>coordinate arts programmes</li>
<li>coordinate sound and lighting, stage management and security, box office sales, distribution and sale of publications, public relations and catering</li>
<li>take part in the commissioning and purchasing of works of art</li>
<li>organise and promote exhibitions and events</li>
<li>help to develop and implement the organisation's strategic plans and marketing strategies</li>
<li>manage the organisation's human and financial resources, including budget preparation</li>
<li>provide an appropriate working environment for employees</li>
<li>liaise with the media</li>
<li>provide education services to the public</li>
<li>make recommendations on cultural grants</li>
<li>ensure compliance with corporate and legal requirements</li>
<li>provide support in policy development and report to the board of directors.</li>

<h2>Requirements for Skills Assessment</h2>
<p>Arts Administrator or Manager is classified as Group B occupations by Vetassess. The criteria for skills assessment of Group B occupations are the following :</p>
<li>Qualification(s) assessed at AQF Bachelor degree or higher degree with a highly relevant field of study and at least one year of post-qualification highly relevant employment in the last five years; </li>
<li>Qualification(s) assessed at AQF Bachelor degree or higher degree with an additional qualification at least Australian Qualifications Framework (AQF) Diploma level in a highly relevant field of study and at least two years of post qualification highly relevant employment in the last five years; </li>
<li>Qualification(s) assessed at AQF Bachelor degree or higher degree without a highly relevant field of study and at least three years of post-qualification highly relevant employment in the last five years;</li>
<p>According to Vetassess, if you have pre-qualification employment and if all of your employment is before your qualification assessed at the AQF bachelor degree level , you are required to have five years of highly relevant employment in addition to at least one year of highly relevant employment at an appropriate skill level completed in the last five years.</p>
