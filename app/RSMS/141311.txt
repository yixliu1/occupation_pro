<h1>Occupation Name: Hotel or Motel Manager</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 141311</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: VETASSESS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Hotel or Motel Manager organises and controls the operations of a hotel or motel to provide guest accommodation, meals and other services.</p>
<p>Hotel or Motel Manager may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>directing and overseeing reservation, reception, room service and housekeeping activities</li>
<li>supervising security arrangements, and garden and property maintenance</li>
<li>planning and supervising bar, restaurant, function and conference activities</li>
<li>observing liquor, gaming, and other laws and regulations</li>
<li>assessing and reviewing customer satisfaction</li>
<li>overseeing accounting and purchasing activities</li>
<li>ensuring compliance with occupational health and safety regulations</li>
<li>may provide guests with local tourism information, and arrange tours and transportation</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation belongs to Short-term Skilled Occupation List (STSOL) . STSOL consists of the list of the occupations which are high in demand in Australia. If you want to apply for Australia sponsored Permanent Residency (subclass 190) your occupation must be listed in the relevant STSOL list and you must have an invitation/sponsorship from the state government. This list is also a part of the Australia Point system. If eligible, you can live and work in Australia for an indefinite period of time.</p>
