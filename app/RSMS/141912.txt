<h1>Occupation Name: Retirement Village Manager</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 141912</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: VETASSESS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Retirement Village Manager organises and controls the day-to-day operations of a retirement village to provide a range of accommodation, personal care services, and recreational and social activities for the use and enjoyment of residents.</p>
<p>Retirement Village Manager may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>planning and organising special functions, sporting, gaming and entertainment activities</li>
<li>directing and overseeing reservation, reception, room service and housekeeping activities</li>
<li>observing liquor, gaming, health and other laws and regulations</li>
<li>monitoring quality at all stages of preparation and presentation of food and services</li>
<li>controlling the selection, training and supervision of staff</li>
<li>ensuring compliance with occupational health and safety regulations</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation is on the Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme Occupation List (RSMS ROL) , you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclass:</p>
<li>Regional Sponsor Migration Scheme (subclass 187)</li>
