<h1>Occupation Name: Call or Contact Centre Manager</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 149211</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: VETASSESS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Call or Contact Centre Manager organises and controls the operations of a call or contact centre. May work in a call centre.</p>
<p>Call or Contact Centre Manager may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>developing and reviewing policies, programs and procedures concerning customer relations and goods and services provided</li>
<li>ensuring operational efficiency within a call centre</li>
<li>providing direction and feedback to team members and assisting with recruitment</li>
<li>managing, motivating and developing staff providing customer services</li>
<li>planning and implementing after-sales services to follow up customer satisfaction, ensure performance of goods purchased, and modify and improve services provided</li>
<li>liaising with other organisational units, service agents and customers to identify and respond to customer expectations</li>
<li>may work in a call centre</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation is on the Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme Occupation List (RSMS ROL) , you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclass:</p>
<li>Regional Sponsor Migration Scheme (subclass 187)</li>
