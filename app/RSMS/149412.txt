<h1>Occupation Name: Railway Station Manager</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 149412</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: VETASSESS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Railway Station Manager organises and controls the operations of a railway station.</p>
<p>Railway Station Manager may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>organising the purchase and maintenance of transport vehicles, equipment and fuel</li>
<li>liaising with clients to determine requirements and providing customers with advice and information regarding vehicle type, purchase or hire rates and obligations and handling complaints</li>
<li>receiving orders and bookings, and planning and implementing transportation schedules</li>
<li>ensuring goods are stored and transported in conditions that will maintain their quality</li>
<li>arranging collection and delivery of vehicles and goods</li>
<li>maintaining business records and preparing operational statements and reports</li>
<li>coordinating activities associated with the arrival, departure, loading and unloading of trains</li>
<li>ensuring compliance with occupational health and safety regulations</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation is on the Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme Occupation List (RSMS ROL) , you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclass:</p>
<li>Regional Sponsor Migration Scheme (subclass 187)</li>
<li>Skilled Employer Sponsored Regional (Provisional) visa (Subclass 494)</li>
<h2>Requirements for Skills Assessment</h2>
<p>Railway Station Manager is classified as Group C occupations by Vetassess. The criteria for skills assessment of Group C occupations are the following :</p>
<li>Qualification(s) assessed at least AQF Diploma level with a highly relevant field of study and at least one year of post qualification highly relevant employment in the last five years; </li>
<li>Qualification(s) assessed at least AQF Diploma level with an additional qualification at least AQF Certificate IV in a highly relevant field of study and at least one year of post-qualification highly relevant employment in the last five years; </li>
<li>Qualification(s) assessed at least AQF Diploma level without a highly relevant field of study and at least two years of post-qualification highly relevant employment in the last five years;</li>
<p>According to Vetassess, if you have pre-qualification employment and if all of your employment is before your qualification assessed at the AQF diploma level, three years of highly relevant employment in addition to at least one year of highly relevant employment completed in the last five years.</p>
