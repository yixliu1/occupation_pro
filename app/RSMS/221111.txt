<h1>Occupation Name: Accountant (general)</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 221111</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: CAANZ/CPAA/IPA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Accountant (general) plans and provides systems and services relating to the financial dealings of organisations and individuals, and advises on associated record-keeping and compliance requirements. Registration or licensing is required.</p>
<p>Accountants may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>assist with the formulation of budgetary and accounting policies</li>
<li>prepare financial statements for presentation to boards of directors, management, shareholders and statutory bodies</li>
<li>conduct financial investigations, undertake audits, prepare reports and advise on such matters as the purchase and sale of businesses, mergers, financing, suspected fraud, insolvency and taxation</li>
<li>examine the income and expenditure of institutions</li>
<li>provide assurance about the accuracy of information contained in financial reports and their compliance with statutory requirements</li>
<li>provide financial and taxation advice about business structures, plans and operations</li>
<li>liaise with bankers and brokers to establish funds management arrangements</li>
<li>advise on the selection and application of computer-based accounting systems</li>
<li>appraise cash flow and financial risk of investment projects.</li>
