<h1>Occupation Name: Internal Auditor</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 221214</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: VETASSESS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Internal Auditor examines, verifies, evaluates and reports on financial, operational and managerial processes, systems and outcomes to ensure financial and operational integrity and compliance, and assists in business process reviews, risk assessments, developing deliverables and reporting progress against outcomes. Registration or licensing is required.</p>
<p>Auditors may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>give an independent opinion on the financial performance of an organisation, and the extent to which its obligations are met</li>
<li>verify the accuracy of information in the accounting records</li>
<li>verify that assets and liabilities mentioned in reports in fact exist</li>
<li>prepare financial statements and reports for senior management, shareholders and statutory bodies</li>
<li>arrange pre-audit meetings with taxpayers to discuss the scope and process of the audit</li>
<li>prepare audit reports, and follow up on actions and issues arising from these reports</li>
<li>assess the efficiency and effectiveness of an organisation, be it a private company or a government department</li>
<li>examine the records of government departments, statutory and municipal authorities and government-owned enterprises</li>
<li>advise clients on ways to improve their accounting systems, policies and procedures throughout the audit process</li>
<li>assist in a business stocktake and valuation of a business</li>
<li>follow up on actions required as a result of report findings.</li>
