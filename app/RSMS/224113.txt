<h1>Occupation Name: Statistician</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 224113</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: VETASSESS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Statistician designs and applies statistical principles and techniques for collecting, organising and interpreting quantifiable data, and uses statistical methodologies to produce statistical reports and analyses for government, commercial and other purposes.</p>
<p>Statisticians may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>define precisely what is to be measured and decide which information to collect and from where</li>
<li>determine the size of the sample to be tested and how the sample will be selected</li>
<li>decide on the best method of collecting the information, whether by mail-out of questionnaires, experiments or survey interviews (telephone or face-to-face), and create associated measurement tools</li>
<li>ensure measurement resources are used efficiently</li>
<li>interpret data and produce relevant statistics to describe or infer particular trends and patterns</li>
<li>model and predict trends for phenomena such as accidents, pollution, disease outbreaks and economic trends</li>
<li>check the validity and reliability of statistical models used in applications</li>
<li>fit models using statistical computing environments and packages and develop, code and run statistical computer programmes</li>
<li>design experiments and trials to test new methods and products</li>
<li>evaluate the accuracy of the obtained information, represent data graphically and report findings.</li>
