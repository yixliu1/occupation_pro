<h1>ANZSCO Code: 224512</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: VETASSESS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Valuer assesses the value of land, property, commercial equipment, merchandise, personal effects, household goods and objects of art. Registration or licensing may be required.</p>
<p>Valuers may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>examine items and select appropriate methods of evaluation</li>
<li>calculate values by considering market supply and demand, condition of items, future trends and other factors (location, size, topography, zoning legislation, and the availability of services, such as transport, community facilities and parking, in the case of real estate, for example)</li>
<li>submit written assessments of value</li>
<li>provide consultancy advice about valuation matters</li>
<li>give evidence in legal proceedings and mediate valuation matters</li>
<li>provide rental valuations for arbitration purposes.</li>
