<h1>Occupation Name: Librarian</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 224611</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: VETASSESS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Librarian develops, organises and manages library services such as collections of information, recreational resources and reader information services. Registration or licensing may be required.</p>
<p>Librarians may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>select, classify and index library and information resources</li>
<li>develop, manage and digitise collections</li>
<li>assist users to identify or interpret information</li>
<li>conduct training programmes to assist library users with their information searches</li>
<li>communicate and conduct library services through websites and social media</li>
<li>train and supervise other staff</li>
<li>create and maintain databases</li>
<li>develop relationships with communities, such as faculty, not-for-profit organisations, learning providers, authors and publishers</li>
<li>be involved in the planning and selection of computer systems for use within the library.</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation belongs to Short-term Skilled Occupation List (STSOL) . STSOL consists of the list of the occupations which are high in demand in Australia. If you want to apply for Australia sponsored Permanent Residency (subclass 190) your occupation must be listed in the relevant STSOL list and you must have an invitation/sponsorship from the state government. This list is also a part of the Australia Point system. If eligible, you can live and work in Australia for an indefinite period of time.</p>
<p>If your occupation is on STSOL, you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclasses:</p>
<li>Skilled Nominated visa (subclass 190)</li>
<li>Training visa (subclass 407)</li>
<li>Skilled Work Regional (Provisional) visa (subclass 491) - State or Territory nominated</li>
<li>Temporary Skill Shortage visa (subclass 482) - Short-term stream</li>
<li>Skilled Employer Sponsored Regional (Provisional) visa (Subclass 494)</li>
<h2>Requirements for Skills Assessment</h2>
<p>Librarian is classified as Group A occupations by Vetassess. The criteria for skills assessment of Group A occupations is to hold qualification(s) assessed at Australian Bachelor degree or higher degree with a highly relevant field of study and at least one year of post-qualification highly relevant employment in the last five years.</p>
