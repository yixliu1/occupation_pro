<h1>Occupation Name: Management Consultant</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 224711</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: VETASSESS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Management Consultant assists organisations to achieve greater efficiency and solve organisational problems.</p>
<p>Management consultants may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>gather information on the function of each area within an organisation (and the specific duties undertaken by each individual) by talking with managers or supervisors and interviewing other staff</li>
<li>assess the types of tasks undertaken in each section, the methods or systems used, the length of time taken for an individual to complete a task and any problems involved in undertaking the task</li>
<li>consult internal training manuals, files and job descriptions to obtain additional information</li>
<li>study data such as statistical records, reports and organisational charts</li>
<li>identify problem areas and find solutions by considering alternatives, taking into account the benefits and costs of any recommendations</li>
<li>write detailed reports and provide statistical evidence to support their conclusions and recommendations</li>
<li>assist in the implementation of new systems</li>
<li>talk to experts in other fields when introducing new equipment such as computers.</li>

<h2>Requirements for Skills Assessment</h2>
<p>Management Consultant is classified as Group B occupations by Vetassess. The criteria for skills assessment of Group B occupations are the following :</p>
<li>Qualification(s) assessed at AQF Bachelor degree or higher degree with a highly relevant field of study and at least one year of post-qualification highly relevant employment in the last five years; </li>
<li>Qualification(s) assessed at AQF Bachelor degree or higher degree with an additional qualification at least Australian Qualifications Framework (AQF) Diploma level in a highly relevant field of study and at least two years of post qualification highly relevant employment in the last five years; </li>
<li>Qualification(s) assessed at AQF Bachelor degree or higher degree without a highly relevant field of study and at least three years of post-qualification highly relevant employment in the last five years;</li>
<p>According to Vetassess, if you have pre-qualification employment and if all of your employment is before your qualification assessed at the AQF bachelor degree level , you are required to have five years of highly relevant employment in addition to at least one year of highly relevant employment at an appropriate skill level completed in the last five years.</p>
