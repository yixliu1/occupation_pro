<h1>Occupation Name: Air Transport Professionals nec</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 231199</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: VETASSESS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>This occupation group covers Air Transport Professionals not elsewhere classified. Occupations in this group include: Aircraft, Navigator, Air Observer (Rescue), Airworthiness Surveyor, Balloonist, Flight Engineer Inspector, Navigator (Air Force)</p>
<p>Air Transport Professionals may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>preparing and submitting flight plans giving consideration to factors such as weather conditions and aircraft performance</li>
<li>flying aircraft in accordance with established air traffic control and aircraft operating procedures</li>
<li>providing flight information for flight crews and air traffic services staff</li>
<li>controlling aircraft movements, and directing aircraft taxiing, take-offs and landings by radio</li>
<li>providing pre-flight briefings and aeronautical information services</li>
<li>completing cockpit preparations and external inspections to determine that aircraft are acceptable for flight</li>
<li>monitoring aircraft performance and reporting on mechanical condition</li>
<li>giving in-flight instruction, supervising solo flights, accompanying students on training flights and demonstrating techniques for controlling aircraft</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation is on the Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme Occupation List (RSMS ROL) , you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclass:</p>
<li>Regional Sponsor Migration Scheme (subclass 187)</li>
<li>Skilled Employer Sponsored Regional (Provisional) visa (Subclass 494)</li>
<h2>Requirements for Skills Assessment</h2>
<p>Air Transport Professional is classified as Group B occupations by Vetassess. The criteria for skills assessment of Group B occupations are the following :</p>
<li>Qualification(s) assessed at AQF Bachelor degree or higher degree with a highly relevant field of study and at least one year of post-qualification highly relevant employment in the last five years; </li>
<li>Qualification(s) assessed at AQF Bachelor degree or higher degree with an additional qualification at least Australian Qualifications Framework (AQF) Diploma level in a highly relevant field of study and at least two years of post qualification highly relevant employment in the last five years; </li>
<li>Qualification(s) assessed at AQF Bachelor degree or higher degree without a highly relevant field of study and at least three years of post-qualification highly relevant employment in the last five years;</li>
