<h1>Occupation Name: Ship's Officer</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 231214</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: AMSA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Ship's Officer navigates and controls the safe operation of a ship and supervises and coordinates the activities of deck crew. Registration or licensing is required.</p>
<p>Ships' officers may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>liaise between the master and the crew of the ship</li>
<li>keep watch from the bridge in watches (shifts)</li>
<li>determine the position of the ship and other vessels and obstructions by using navigational aids</li>
<li>supervise the work done by the crew</li>
<li>provide medical and/or first aid treatment for the crew</li>
<li>make sure safety regulations are observed</li>
<li>perform designated safety and firefighting drills in emergency situations</li>
<li>observe and record the ship's course and any alterations, the weather conditions and the ship's position in the daily logbook</li>
<li>observe the ship's on-board security procedures and take part in security drills</li>
<li>coordinate cargo loading and discharge operations and maintenance</li>
<li>update charts and other nautical publications</li>
<li>ensure that all necessary documentation has been completed.</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
