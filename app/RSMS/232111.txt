<h1>Occupation Name: Architect</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 232111</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: AACA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Architect plans and designs buildings, provides concepts, plans, specifications and detailed drawings, negotiates with builders and advises on the procurement of buildings. Registration or licensing is required.</p>
<p>Architects may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>discuss requirements of clients or builders (to design a new structure or modify an existing one) and prepare a brief</li>
<li>prepare sketches, production drawings and detailed drawings by hand or using computer-aided design (CAD) software</li>
<li>combine structural, mechanical and artistic elements into the building design, such as decks and atriums, lifts, air conditioning systems and decorative finishes</li>
<li>discuss designs and cost estimates with clients and others involved in the project, including engineers, quantity surveyors, landscape architects, and urban and regional planners</li>
<li>obtain necessary approvals from authorities</li>
<li>prepare specifications and contract documents for builders, tradespeople and legal advisers, specifying building materials, construction equipment and, in some cases, the interior furnishings</li>
<li>observe, inspect and monitor building work to ensure it is progressing according to the contract and specifications</li>
<li>evaluate projects once they are completed and occupied.</li>
