<h1>Occupation Name: Surveyor</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 232212</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: SSSI</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Surveyor plans, directs and conducts survey work to determine, delineate, plan and precisely position tracts of land, natural and constructed features, coastlines, marine floors and underground works, and manages related information systems. Registration or licensing may be required.</p>
<p>Surveyors may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>measure the size and shape of an area of land using specialised tools and technology</li>
<li>determine the position of boundaries of public or private land</li>
<li>create property titles through plans for subdivision</li>
<li>compile and evaluate data gathered from field studies</li>
<li>interpret codes and laws to provide professional advice about legal matters relating to boundaries and titles</li>
<li>study the natural and social environment, measure land and marine resources, and use the data in planning development of property, land and buildings in urban, rural and regional areas</li>
<li>work with architects, engineers and developers to plan and monitor construction projects</li>
<li>produce plans, maps, files, charts and reports.</li>
