<h1>Occupation Name: Materials Engineer</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 233112</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: Engineers Australia</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Materials Engineer investigates the properties of metals, ceramics, polymers and other materials and assesses and develops their engineering and commercial applications. Registration or licensing may be required.</p>
<p>Materials engineers may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>study the structure and properties of metals and other materials</li>
<li>develop methods for processing minerals through to manufactured materials</li>
<li>investigate methods for shaping and fabricating materials, joining materials, improving existing materials and evaluating new ones</li>
<li>participate in the design of products using advanced materials</li>
<li>investigate material failures</li>
<li>study ways to extend the life of materials</li>
<li>establish procedures and methods to protect materials against deterioration and to assess their durability</li>
<li>study possible improvements to production methods, report on the nature and uses of materials and advise on manufacturing techniques and quality control systems.</li>
