<h1>Occupation Name: Quantity Surveyor</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 233213</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: AIQS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Quantity Surveyor estimates and monitors construction costs from the project feasibility stage, through tender preparation, to the construction period and beyond. Registration or licensing is required.</p>
<p>Quantity surveyors may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>liaise with architects, engineers, builders, contractors, suppliers and project owners</li>
<li>study architectural and engineering drawings and specifications</li>
<li>prepare a 'Bill of Quantities', which lists the individual components required to construct the project</li>
<li>check on changes of design to assess the effects on cost</li>
<li>assess and recommend payment to contractors during construction</li>
<li>prepare monthly cash-flow forecasts for clients and tax depreciation schedules</li>
<li>undertake feasibility studies to assist with decisions about the worth of a project proceeding</li>
<li>act as consultants to business and government.</li>
