<h1>Occupation Name: Electrical Engineer</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 233311</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: Engineers Australia</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Electrical Engineer designs, develops and supervises the manufacture, installation, operation and maintenance of equipment, machines and systems for the generation, distribution, utilisation and control of electric power. Registration or licensing may be required.</p>
<p>Electrical engineers may perform the following tasks: </p>
<li>plan and design power stations and equipment for generators</li>
<li>supervise construction plans and specifications and draw up contracts</li>
<li>supervise operating and maintenance staff</li>
<li>design and produce drawings of electrical systems using computer-aided design (CAD)</li>
<li>decide on the type and arrangement of circuits, transformers, circuit-breakers, transmission lines and equipment, based on calculations</li>
<li>make or improve products such as electric motors, parts, equipment and appliances</li>
<li>prepare and interpret specifications, drawings and regulations for the use of electric power equipment</li>
<li>determine delivery and installation schedules for machines, switchgear, cables and fittings</li>
<li>organise and manage staff and materials in the making of electrical parts, machines, appliances and equipment</li>
<li>ensure completed works meet specifications and safety standards</li>
<li>design and install control and signalling devices for road, rail and air traffic</li>
<li>design telecommunications equipment and networks</li>
<li>contribute and adhere to safety requirements.</li>
