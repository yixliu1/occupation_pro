<h1>Occupation Name: Electronics Engineer</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 233411</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: Engineers Australia</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Electronics Engineer designs, develops, adapts, installs, tests and maintains electronic components, circuits and systems used for computer systems, communication systems, entertainment, transport and other industrial applications.</p>
<p>Electronics engineers may perform the following tasks: </p>
<li>design circuits for electronic control systems and instrumentation</li>
<li>prepare and supervise designs, specifications, estimates, tenders and contracts</li>
<li>programme and operate computers to assist with complex calculations</li>
<li>determine the type and arrangement of circuit parts and develop testing equipment and methods</li>
<li>determine the type of installation, location, layout and transmission medium by assessing communication traffic and levels of service at installations</li>
<li>determine and monitor performance, safety standards and methods for modification, maintenance and repair</li>
<li>check installations to ensure they meet contract conditions</li>
<li>research new applications of technology</li>
<li>talk to clients, other engineers, technical officers, technicians, tradespeople and other workers.</li>
