<h1>Occupation Name: Mechanical Engineer</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 233512</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: Engineers Australia</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Mechanical Engineer plans, designs, organises and oversees the assembly, erection, operation and maintenance of mechanical and process plant and installations. Registration or licensing may be required.</p>
<p>Mechanical engineers may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>design new machines, equipment or systems, taking into account cost, availability of materials, and strength and maintenance requirements</li>
<li>consider the appearance of the designs as well as the impact on the users and on the environment</li>
<li>carry out research on the use of different types of fuel and energy, materials handling, heating and cooling processes, the storage and pumping of liquids and gases, and environmental controls</li>
<li>use computer-aided design (CAD) to assist with design and drawing</li>
<li>design and construct resource development projects such as offshore platforms, onshore gas plants and iron ore mining facilities</li>
<li>supervise the operation of manufacturing process plants such as vehicle and electrical appliance production plants, coal handling installations, power stations, and sewerage and water supply pumping stations</li>
<li>specify, select and install factory machinery and production systems and manage machinery maintenance</li>
<li>set up work control systems (such as the testing of equipment) to ensure that performance, quality, cost and safety requirements are met</li>
<li>act as consultants, researching possible changes or improvements and estimating costs of products for clients.</li>
