<h1>Occupation Name: Engineering Technologist</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 233914</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: Engineers Australia</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Engineering Technologist analyses and modifies new and existing engineering technologies and applies them in the testing and implementation of engineering projects. Registration or licensing may be required.</p>
<p>Engineering technologists may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>estimate costs and prepare specifications for materials used in engineering works</li>
<li>assist in establishing detailed programmes for the planning of the design and construction processes</li>
<li>communicate with project managers and construction workers</li>
<li>organise the delivery of materials, plant machinery and equipment needed for construction</li>
<li>supervise labour</li>
<li>analyse and record reports on loading, labour, productivity, quality, materials and performance</li>
<li>operate and programme computers to assist with calculations</li>
<li>measure and test materials, processes or systems related to engineering works</li>
<li>inspect engineering works, and supervise repair work and maintenance</li>
<li>check to ensure that finished works are within specifications, contract terms and regulations.</li>
