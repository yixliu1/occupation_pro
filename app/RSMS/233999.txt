<h1>Occupation Name: Engineering Professionals (nec)</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 233999</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: Engineers Australia</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>This occupation group covers Engineering Professionals not elsewhere classified. Occupations in this group include: </p>
<p>Mechatronics Engineer</p>
<p>, </p>
<p>Product Design Engineer.</p>
<p>Mechatronics Engineer may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>design, develop, maintain and manage high technology engineering systems for the automation of industrial tasks</li>
<li>apply mechatronic or automated solutions to the transfer of material, components or finished goods</li>
<li>apply advanced electronic control systems, which are usually computer-driven</li>
<li>design and assist with the manufacture of consumer products such as cameras and video recorders</li>
<li>apply electronic and mechanical processes and computers to tasks where the use of human labour may be dangerous (underwater exploration, mining or forestry, for example)</li>
<li>carry out studies into the feasibility, cost implications and performance benefits of new mechatronic equipment.</li>
