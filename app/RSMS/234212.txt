<h1>Occupation Name: Food Technologist</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 234212</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: VETASSESS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Food Technologist develops new and improves existing food products, and sets standards for producing, packaging and marketing food.</p>
<p>Food technologists may perform the following tasks: </p>
<li>ensure safe and hygienic conditions are maintained during processing, storage and packaging of food</li>
<li>check raw ingredients and processed food for nutritional value, safety and quality</li>
<li>undertake basic and applied research into aspects of food processing, food preservation, food quality, food deterioration, packaging, storage and delivery</li>
<li>check foods for colour, texture and taste to ensure products meet government regulations and standards</li>
<li>develop and ensure food standards</li>
<li>demonstrate products to clients</li>
<li>develop new products and devise the techniques needed to produce these foods and maintain quality during storage</li>
<li>supervise cleaning and maintenance of machinery used in food processing</li>
<li>compare products with those of other brands and participate in surveys that provide information to management about new products and market trends</li>
<li>supervise the transportation of foodstuffs such as fruit, vegetables and milk, as well as inspect for spoilage and quality deterioration</li>
<li>develop quality control procedures for the manufacture of products in plants or factories</li>
<li>analyse results and experimental data</li>
<li>supervise and coordinate the work of technicians.</li>
