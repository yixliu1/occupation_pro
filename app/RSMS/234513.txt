<h1>Occupation Name: Biochemist</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 234513</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: VETASSESS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Biochemist studies the biochemistry of living organisms and the molecular structure and function of related components.</p>
<p>Biochemists may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>study the chemical processes that occur within individual cells</li>
<li>study processes that involve whole organisms, such as digestion and growth</li>
<li>undertake detailed chemical analysis using sophisticated instruments and techniques</li>
<li>prepare, or supervise the preparation of, scientific reports and papers for journals based on observations and experiments.</li>
