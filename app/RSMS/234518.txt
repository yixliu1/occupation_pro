<h1>Occupation Name: Zoologist</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 234518</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: VETASSESS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Zoologist studies the anatomy, physiology, characteristics, ecology, behaviour and environments of animals.</p>
<p>Zoologists may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>investigate the interrelationships between animals and their environment by studying animals in their natural surroundings, as well as in captivity and laboratories</li>
<li>study and perform experiments to identify species and gather data on growth, nutrition, reproduction, prey and predators</li>
<li>study the development and functions of animals</li>
<li>devise methods of population control, especially of vermin and pests</li>
<li>develop programmes to increase or manage the population of wild animals and animals in captivity</li>
<li>undertake surveys of fauna and prepare reports for management agencies</li>
<li>prepare or supervise the preparation of laboratory reports and scientific papers</li>
<li>supervise and coordinate the work of technical officers and technicians</li>
<li>conduct lectures at tertiary institutions</li>
<li>participate in wildlife awareness programmes for the public.</li>
