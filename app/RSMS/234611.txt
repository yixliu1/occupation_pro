<h1>Occupation Name: Medical Laboratory Scientist</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 234611</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: AIMS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Medical Laboratory Scientist conducts medical laboratory tests to assist in the diagnosis, treatment and prevention of disease.</p>
<p>Medical Laboratory Scientist may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>preparing tissue sections for microscopic examination</li>
<li>examining and analysing samples to study the effects of microbial infections</li>
<li>analysing samples of body tissue and fluids to develop techniques to aid in the diagnosis and treatment of diseases</li>
<li>advising Medical Practitioners on the interpretation of tests and methods for use in the diagnosis and treatment of disease</li>
<li>setting up the steps and rules of laboratory medical testing</li>
<li>operating and maintaining laboratory equipment</li>
<li>maintaining laboratory quality assurance and safety standards</li>
<li>preparing scientific papers and reports</li>
