<h1>Assessment Authorities: AVBC</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Veterinarian diagnoses, treats and prevents animal diseases, ailments and injuries. Registration or licensing is required.</p>
<p>Veterinarians may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>examine sick and injured animals and perform diagnostic tests</li>
<li>examine tissue and samples from sick animals to identify the cause of disease</li>
<li>treat animals by using drugs, surgical procedures and nursing care</li>
<li>vaccinate animals to prevent disease</li>
<li>certify viability of animals for breeding and performance</li>
<li>give advice about animal health and care to owners and breeders</li>
<li>humanely euthanise animals to end pain and suffering or to prevent the spread of disease</li>
<li>advise on feeding and breeding strategies to achieve maximum production</li>
<li>notify authorities of outbreaks of animal diseases and certify animals for exportation</li>
<li>attend horse and greyhound tracks, animal-related sporting events, and dog and cat shows to monitor and/or advise on the condition of animals</li>
<li>supervise the handling and health of livestock destined for export</li>
<li>supervise quarantine for incoming livestock, genetic material and animal products</li>
<li>assist in public education programmes, promoting the welfare of animals.</li>
