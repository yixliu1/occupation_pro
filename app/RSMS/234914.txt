<h1>Occupation Name: Physicist</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 234914</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: VETASSESS / ACPSEM</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Physicists may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>observe and measure phenomena in the physical world, from the smallest subatomic particle through to the universe as a whole</li>
<li>propose theories and models to explain phenomena</li>
<li>use computers to explore the consequences of theories and models</li>
<li>build equipment to make new types of measurements, which in many cases have never been attempted before</li>
<li>create new ways of understanding observations that have been made</li>
<li>develop new materials, products and processes for use in industry, medicine, defence and other areas of research and development.</li>
