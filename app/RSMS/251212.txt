<h1>ANZSCO Code: 251212</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: ASMIRT</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Medical Radiation Therapist Operates high energy X-ray and other radiation and electron generating and monitoring equipment to administer radiation treatment for medical purposes.</p>
<p>Medical Radiation therapists may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>provide explanations and information to patients about radiation therapy treatment, its possible side effects and self-care procedures</li>
<li>coordinate the various activities that make up the patient's treatment and care plan</li>
<li>use simulators, CT scanners and other medical imaging equipment to identify and define the anatomy to be treated and those to be avoided</li>
<li>devise a treatment plan that will deliver the optimum radiation dose to the target anatomy and minimise dose to unaffected anatomy</li>
<li>calculate the treatment machine settings, associated equipment and computer verification systems to deliver the radiation dose as prescribed by the radiation oncologist</li>
<li>administer the radiation treatment and record the delivered dosage into patients' record sheets</li>
<li>monitor and assess the patient's wellbeing before, during and after the treatment, taking particular note of side effects of treatment</li>
<li>participate in research and development activities and clinical trials</li>
<li>supervise and train students allocated from universities in the practical aspects of radiation therapy</li>
<li>commit to the Continuing Professional Development Program (which is mandatory for registration).</li>
