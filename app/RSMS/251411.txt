<h1>Occupation Name: Optometrist</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 251411</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: OCANZ</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Optometrist performs eye examinations and vision tests to determine the presence of visual, ocular and other abnormalities, ocular diseases and systemic diseases with ocular manifestations, and prescribes lenses, other optical aids, therapy and medication to correct and manage vision problems and eye diseases. Registration or licensing is required.</p>
<p>Optometrists may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>determine the nature and extent of vision and eye problems and disease by examining patients' eyes using specialised instruments, techniques and tests</li>
<li>correct vision defects by prescribing and dispensing glasses or contact lenses, special optical aids or vision therapy</li>
<li>examine patients for signs of eye disease and systemic conditions, including glaucoma, diabetes, high blood pressure and multiple sclerosis, and work with medical practitioners and eye surgeons to co-manage patients when required</li>
<li>manage some eye conditions by prescribing ocular therapeutic drugs</li>
<li>monitor patients' vision and ocular health with the use of imaging techniques such as digital retinal photography and computerised vision tests</li>
<li>conduct eye screening and testing for the purposes of determining occupational fitness or fitness to drive</li>
<li>undertake managerial, retail and administrative tasks.</li>
