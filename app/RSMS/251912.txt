<h1>Occupation Name: Orthotist or Prosthetist</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 251912</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: AOPA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Orthotist or Prosthetist designs, builds, fits and repairs splints, braces, callipers, artificial limbs and related appliances to restore function or compensate for muscular and skeletal disabilities.</p>
<p>Prosthetists/orthotists may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>assess and establish clients' needs through physical examinations and standardised tests</li>
<li>assess clients' functional abilities in home, work, educational and leisure environments and recommend appropriate prosthetic/orthotic devices</li>
<li>design, prescribe and fit prosthetic/orthotic devices to meet clients' needs</li>
<li>provide advice to other healthcare professionals regarding patients' problems, needs, progress and any pre-surgical planning</li>
<li>provide gait (walking) training and education relating to the use of prosthetic/orthotic devices</li>
<li>test and adjust the prostheses or orthoses for effectiveness and client comfort</li>
<li>record and report on client progress</li>
<li>supervise support personnel.</li>
