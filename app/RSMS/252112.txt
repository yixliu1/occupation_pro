<h1>ANZSCO Code: 252112</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: AOAC</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Osteopath diagnoses and treats tissue strains, stresses and dysfunctions which impede normal neural, vascular and biochemical mechanisms, and provides advice on preventing these disorders. Registration or licensing is required.</p>
<p>Osteopaths may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>diagnose patients' complaints using information from case histories, physical examinations, observation of patients' body structure and mobility, and medical imaging and laboratory tests</li>
<li>treat biomechanical complaints (including backache, general joint problems, sports injuries, arthritis, stress and repetitive strain injuries), some breathing problems (such as asthma) and a wide range of general medical complaints (headaches, for example)</li>
<li>treat patients using a variety of manual techniques, such as soft tissue stretching, muscle relaxation, gentle mobilisation or manipulation</li>
<li>treat children for the after-effects of birth trauma and childhood accidents</li>
<li>devise exercise and nutritional programmes.</li>
