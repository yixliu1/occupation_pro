<h1>Occupation Name: Physiotherapist</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 252511</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: APC</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Physiotherapist assesses, treats and prevents disorders in human movement caused by injury or disease. Registration or licensing is required.</p>
<p>Physiotherapists may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>assess the physical condition of patients to diagnose problems and plan appropriate treatment</li>
<li>use a range of techniques to strengthen and stretch muscles and joints to improve patient mobility (such as massage, hydrotherapy, breathing and relaxation techniques)</li>
<li>perform spinal and peripheral joint mobilisation and manipulation</li>
<li>use equipment such as heat packs, ice packs, exercise equipment, ultrasound and electrotherapy to ease pain, reduce swelling and improve range of movement</li>
<li>retrain patients to walk or teach them to use devices such as walking frames, splints, crutches and wheelchairs</li>
<li>educate patients, their families and the community to prevent injury and disability and to lead healthy lifestyles</li>
<li>plan and implement community fitness programmes</li>
<li>maintain patient records.</li>
