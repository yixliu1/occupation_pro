<h1>Occupation Name: Podiatrist</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 252611</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: PodBA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Podiatrist prevents, diagnoses and treats disorder of the feet. Registration or licensing is required.</p>
<p>Podiatrists may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>examine patients' feet to diagnose any disorders or infections and decide on an appropriate treatment</li>
<li>give advice about foot health and the prevention of foot disorders</li>
<li>treat deformities such as flat feet and foot imbalance by using corrective devices such as orthoses</li>
<li>work with patients suffering from diabetes, rheumatoid arthritis, peripheral vascular disorders and other neuropathies</li>
<li>perform surgical procedures on the foot</li>
<li>treat abnormalities of bones, joints, skin and nails such as bunions, toe deformities, ingrown toenails, corns, warts and other infections</li>
<li>recommend footwear for specific foot conditions</li>
<li>prescribe orthotic devices for protection and correction of a wide range of foot and leg irregularities.</li>
