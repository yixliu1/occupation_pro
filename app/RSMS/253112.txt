<h1>Occupation Name: Resident Medical Officer</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 253112</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: MedBA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Resident Medical Officer diagnoses, treats and prevents human physical and mental disorders and injuries under the supervision of medical specialists or senior general practitioners. Registration or licensing is required.</p>
<p>Resident Medical Officer may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>conducting examinations and questioning patients to determine the nature of disorders and illnesses, and recording patients' medical information</li>
<li>ordering laboratory tests, X-rays and other diagnostic procedures, and interpreting findings to assist in diagnosis</li>
<li>providing overall care for patients, and prescribing and administering treatments, medications and other remedial measures</li>
<li>monitoring patients' progress and response to treatment</li>
<li>advising on diet, exercise and other habits which aid prevention and treatment of disease and disorders</li>
<li>referring patients to, and exchanging medical information with, specialist Medical Practitioners</li>
<li>reporting births, deaths and notifiable diseases to government authorities</li>
<li>arranging the admission of patients to hospitals</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation belongs to Short-term Skilled Occupation List (STSOL) . STSOL consists of the list of the occupations which are high in demand in Australia. If you want to apply for Australia sponsored Permanent Residency (subclass 190) your occupation must be listed in the relevant STSOL list and you must have an invitation/sponsorship from the state government. This list is also a part of the Australia Point system. If eligible, you can live and work in Australia for an indefinite period of time.</p>
<p>If your occupation is on STSOL, you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclasses:</p>
<li>Skilled Nominated visa (subclass 190)</li>
<li>Training visa (subclass 407)</li>
<li>Skilled Work Regional (Provisional) visa (subclass 491) - State or Territory nominated</li>
<li>Temporary Skill Shortage visa (subclass 482) - Short-term stream</li>
<li>Skilled Employer Sponsored Regional (Provisional) visa (Subclass 494)</li>
