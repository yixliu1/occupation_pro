<h1>Occupation Name: Specialist Physician (General Medicine)</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 253311</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: MedBA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Specialist Physician (General Medicine) investigates and diagnoses internal human disorders and diseases, and administers treatment.</p>
<p>Specialist Physician (General Medicine) may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>examining patients to determine the nature and extent of problems after referral from General Medical Practitioners and other medical specialists, and undertaking laboratory tests and diagnostic procedures</li>
<li>analysing test results and other medical information to make diagnoses</li>
<li>prescribing and administering drugs, and remedial and therapeutic treatment and procedures</li>
<li>recording medical information and data</li>
<li>reporting specified contagious and notifiable diseases to government health and immigration authorities</li>
<li>may admit or refer patients to hospitals</li>
<li>may consult other medical specialists</li>
