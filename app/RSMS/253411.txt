<h1>ANZSCO Code: 253411</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: MedBA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Psychiatrist diagnoses, assesses, treats and prevents human mental, emotional and behavioural disorders. Registration or licensing is required.</p>
<p>Psychiatrists may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>talk to patients about their mental, emotional or behavioural problems in order to work out the nature and extent of these disorders</li>
<li>study patients' medical and mental histories</li>
<li>examine patients to assess their general physical condition</li>
<li>order laboratory tests, imaging, assessment tests and other diagnostic procedures</li>
<li>look at the results of tests and examinations to work out the most appropriate forms of treatment</li>
<li>prescribe and administer medication, psychotherapy or other treatment and rehabilitation programs, and arrange admission to hospitals</li>
<li>consult and work with other health professionals.</li>
