<h1>ANZSCO Code: 253913</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: MedBA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Obstetrician and Gynaecologist provides diagnostic, treatment and preventative medical and surgical services related to the care of women, foetuses and children during pregnancy and childbirth, and to disorders of the female genital, urinary, rectal and reproductive organs. Registration or licensing is required.</p>
<p>Obstetrician and Gynaecologist may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>examining patients to establish the nature of their complaints, and performing and ordering tests, X-rays and other diagnostic procedures</li>
<li>determining diagnosis based on examination and results of tests</li>
<li>selecting and administering appropriate treatments and therapies, and advising patients of further treatment options and preventative and therapeutic measures</li>
<li>prescribing, administering, preparing and dispensing medication and prosthetic and corrective devices</li>
<li>monitoring patients' progress and response to treatment</li>
<li>recording patients' illnesses, treatment given and patients' responses and progress</li>
<li>advising on diet, exercise and other measures to prevent and aid treatment of diseases and disorders</li>
