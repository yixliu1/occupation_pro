<h1>Occupation Name: Midwife</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 254111</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: ANMAC</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Midwife provides care and advice to women during pregnancy, labour and childbirth, and postnatal care for women and babies in a range of settings such as the home, community, hospitals, clinics and health units. Registration or licensing is required.</p>
<p>Midwife may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>providing advice and support during pre-conception, intrapartum, antenatal and postnatal periods in partnership with women</li>
<li>providing care and management of pregnancy and birth</li>
<li>assessing progress and recognising warning signs of abnormal and potentially abnormal pregnancies requiring referral to an Obstetrician</li>
<li>monitoring the condition of women and foetuses during pregnancy and throughout labour</li>
<li>conducting health education classes and seminars to promote the health of mothers and babies such as reproductive health, antenatal education, preparation for parenthood and breastfeeding</li>
<li>providing advice on nutrition, childcare and family planning</li>
