<h1>Occupation Name: Systems Analyst</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 261112</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: ACS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Systems Analyst evaluates processes and methods used in existing ICT systems, proposes modifications, additional system components or new systems to meet user needs as expressed in specifications and other documentation.</p>
<p>Systems Analyst may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>working with users to formulate and document business requirements</li>
<li>identifying, investigating, and analysing business processes, procedures and work practices</li>
<li>identifying and evaluating inefficiencies and recommending optimal business practices, and system functionality and behaviour</li>
<li>using project management methodologies, principles and techniques to develop project plans and to cost, resource and manage projects</li>
<li>taking responsibility for deploying functional solutions, such as creating, adopting and implementing system test plans, which ensure acceptable quality and integrity of the system</li>
<li>creating user and training documentation, and conducting formal training classes</li>
<li>developing functional specifications for use by system developers</li>
<li>using data and process modelling techniques to create clear system specifications for the design and development of system software</li>
<li>acting as a central reference and information source, providing guidance and assistance in the system project decision making process</li>
