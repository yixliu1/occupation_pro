<h1>Occupation Name: Barrister</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 271111</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: Law Society of  a State</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Barrister pleads cases before civil, criminal and industrial courts and other tribunals. Registration or licensing is required.</p>
<p>Barrister may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>receiving written information in the form of briefs and verbal instructions concerning cases from Solicitors, other specialist Legal Professionals and clients</li>
<li>providing advice and written opinions on points of law</li>
<li>conferring with clients and witnesses in preparation for court proceedings</li>
<li>drawing up pleadings, affidavits and other court documents</li>
<li>researching statutes and previous court decisions relevant to cases</li>
<li>outlining the facts to the court, calling and questioning witnesses, and making addresses to the court to argue a client's case</li>
<li>providing opinion on complex legal issues</li>
<li>may draw up or settle documents</li>
