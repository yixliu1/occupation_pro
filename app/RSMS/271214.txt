<h1>Occupation Name: Intellectual Property Lawyer</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 271214</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: VETASSESS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Intellectual Property Lawyer provides legal advice, prepares and drafts legal documents, and conducts negotiations on behalf of clients on matters associated with protecting intellectual capital, utilising patent law, copyright law and licensing. Registration or licensing is required.</p>
<p>Intellectual Property Lawyer may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>receiving written information in the form of briefs and verbal instructions concerning legal cases</li>
<li>researching statutes and previous court decisions relevant to cases</li>
<li>providing advice and written opinions on points of law</li>
<li>interviewing clients, and recommending and undertaking appropriate legal action</li>
<li>preparing cases for court by conducting investigations, undertaking research, arranging witnesses, and giving notice of court actions</li>
<li>representing clients in court, outlining the facts to the court, calling and questioning witnesses, and making addresses to the court to argue a client's case</li>
<li>presiding over judicial proceedings and pronouncing judgments in courts of law</li>
<li>acting individually and as members of administrative and industrial tribunals to resolve disputes</li>
