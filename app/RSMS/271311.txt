<h1>Occupation Name: Solicitor</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 271311</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: Law Society of  a State</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Solicitor provides legal advice, prepares and drafts legal documents, and conducts negotiations on behalf of clients on matters associated with the law. Registration or licensing is required.</p>
<p>Solicitor may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>interviewing clients to determine the nature of problems, and recommending and undertaking appropriate legal action</li>
<li>preparing cases for court by conducting investigations, undertaking research, arranging witness preparation and attendance, and giving notice of court actions</li>
<li>representing clients in court</li>
<li>managing conveyancing and other property matters by preparing contracts of sale, mortgage documents, lease documents and other documents relating to the transfer of land and buildings</li>
<li>preparing and critically reviewing contracts between parties</li>
<li>preparing wills</li>
<li>providing advice on family law, company law, partnerships, commercial law and trusts</li>
<li>may act as trustee or guardian</li>
<li>may act as executor of clients' wills</li>
