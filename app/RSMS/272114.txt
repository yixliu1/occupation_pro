<h1>Occupation Name: Rehabilitation Counsellor</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 272114</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: VETASSESS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Rehabilitation Counsellor assists physically, mentally and socially disadvantaged people to reintegrate into work and the community.</p>
<p>Rehabilitation counsellors may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>counsel clients adjusting to disability</li>
<li>undertake comprehensive assessment of the client's vocational, social, medical, educational, personal and psychological needs in order to develop an individual rehabilitation plan</li>
<li>plan, develop, implement and monitor individual client rehabilitation programmes, ensuring appropriate services are provided within these programmes</li>
<li>refer clients for medical examinations and psychological or psychiatric evaluations</li>
<li>use a variety of assessment tools, tests and strategies to assess a client's particular interests, abilities, skills, motivation, attitude, values and knowledge in order to establish life or vocational goals</li>
<li>provide career development services to clients</li>
<li>analyse and use labour market information when making decisions about a client's vocational choices</li>
<li>arrange formal or on-the-job training for clients as part of their rehabilitation goals</li>
<li>check a client's progress in training or employment phases of the rehabilitation programme</li>
<li>evaluate the effectiveness of redeployment and recommend further action</li>
<li>manage the placement of clients into suitable paid employment or back into the community after coordinating the client's required training</li>
<li>negotiate and manage a gradual return or introduction to a job to ensure a smooth transition</li>
<li>talk to insurance companies, medical practitioners, community organisations and other professionals to ensure all parties are consulted regarding the client's individual rehabilitation programme</li>
<li>provide an information and advisory service to organisations that assist people with disability or social disadvantage.</li>

<p>Rehabilitation Counsellor is classified as Group A occupations by Vetassess.
