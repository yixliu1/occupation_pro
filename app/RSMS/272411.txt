<h1>Occupation Name: Historian</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 272411</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: VETASSESS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Historian researches the history of human activity and prepares accounts of findings.</p>
<p>Historians may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>examine, assess and interpret evidence of past events by consulting and studying the works of other historians and researchers, using sources of information such as archives, diaries, manuscripts, reports, statistics, artefacts, images, works of art, photographs, letters, newspapers, magazines and journals, and interviewing people</li>
<li>organise information, check it for accuracy, interpret details, suggest theories and make conclusions</li>
<li>present findings and conclusions through reports, the media (digital and print), journal articles, books, lectures and museum exhibitions</li>
<li>act as an adviser or consultant to government, commercial and private organisations, societies, churches and individuals</li>
<li>perform research and/or teach at institutions such as universities and schools.</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation is on the Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme Occupation List (RSMS ROL) , you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclass:</p>
<li>Regional Sponsor Migration Scheme (subclass 187)</li>
<li>Skilled Employer Sponsored Regional (Provisional) visa (Subclass 494)</li>
<h2>Requirements for Skills Assessment</h2>
<p>Historian is classified as Group A occupations by Vetassess. The criteria for skills assessment of Group A occupations is to hold qualification(s) assessed at Australian Bachelor degree or higher degree with a highly relevant field of study and at least one year of post-qualification highly relevant employment in the last five years.</p>
