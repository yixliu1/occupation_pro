<h1>Occupation Name: Anaesthetic Technician</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 311211</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: VETASSESS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Anaesthetic Technician prepares and maintains anaesthetic equipment for operating theatres or clinics, and assists Anaesthetists during anaesthetic procedures.</p>
<p>Anaesthetic technicians may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>set up, check and maintain anaesthetic workstations, life support machines and associated equipment</li>
<li>order and prepare medical supplies for anaesthesia</li>
<li>discuss medical procedures with patients and check whether they have any medical conditions that could cause problems under anaesthesia</li>
<li>assist the anaesthetist to insert breathing tubes and intravascular lines in patients</li>
<li>assist the anaesthetist to administer anaesthesia to patients</li>
<li>monitor patients and their vital signs before, during and after procedures</li>
<li>maintain documentation before, during and after surgery</li>
<li>assist with basic life support and post-operative care</li>
<li>assist the anaesthetist in emergencies.</li>
