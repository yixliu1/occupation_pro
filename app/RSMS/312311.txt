<h1>Occupation Name: Electrical Engineering Draftsperson</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 312311</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: Engineers Australia</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Electrical Engineering Draftsperson prepares detailed drawings and plans of electrical installations and circuitry in support of Electrical Engineers and Engineering Technologists. Registration or licensing may be required.</p>
<p>Electrical Engineering Draftsperson may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>preparing drawings, plans and diagrams of electrical installations and circuitry</li>
<li>assisting Electrical Engineers and Engineering Technologists in design and layout of electrical installations and circuitry on substations, switchgear, cabling systems and motor control systems</li>
<li>collecting data, performing tests and complex calculations, graphing results, and preparing charts and tabulations</li>
<li>estimating materials costs and quantities</li>
<li>inspecting designs and finished products for compliance with specifications and regulations</li>
<li>assembling, installing, testing, calibrating, modifying and repairing electrical equipment and installations to conform with regulations and safety requirements</li>
<li>undertaking electrical workshop functions such as installing assemblies for protection relays, metering and indicating devices</li>
<li>assisting with research and experimentation programs</li>
