<h1>Occupation Name: Electronic Engineering Draftsperson</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 312411</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: Engineers Australia</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Electronic Engineering Draftsperson prepares detailed drawings and plans of electronic engineering work in support of Electronics Engineers and Engineering Technologists. Registration or licensing may be required.</p>
<p>Electronic Engineering Draftsperson may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>preparing drawings, plans and diagrams for electronic engineering work</li>
<li>developing, constructing and testing electronic equipment and associated circuitry in accordance with technical manuals and instructions of Electronics Engineers and Engineering Technologists</li>
<li>performing tests, graphing results, preparing charts and tabulations</li>
<li>estimating material costs and quantities</li>
<li>evaluating performance of equipment</li>
<li>inspecting designs and finished products for compliance with specifications, drawings, contracts and regulations</li>
<li>installing, testing, repairing and modifying electronic equipment</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation is on the Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme Occupation List (RSMS ROL) , you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclass:</p>
<li>Regional Sponsor Migration Scheme (subclass 187)</li>
