<h1>Occupation Name: Mechanical Engineering Draftsperson</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 312511</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: Engineers Australia</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Mechanical Engineering Draftsperson prepares detailed drawings and plans of mechanical engineering work in support of Mechanical Engineers and Engineering Technologists.</p>
<p>Mechanical Engineering Draftsperson may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>preparing drawings, plans and designs for mechanical engineering work under the direction of Mechanical Engineers and Engineering Technologists</li>
<li>assisting Mechanical Engineers and Engineering Technologists in the design of mechanical equipment and plant</li>
<li>selecting tools and equipment</li>
<li>assembling and installing new and modified mechanical assemblies, components, machine tools and controls, and hydraulic power systems</li>
<li>estimating material costs and quantities, and machine requirements</li>
<li>performing and directing field and laboratory tests</li>
<li>collecting and analysing data, carrying out complex computations and preparing diagrams</li>
<li>organising and supervising inspection and maintenance of machines and plant</li>
<li>ensuring that designs and finished work are within specifications, regulations and contract provisions</li>
