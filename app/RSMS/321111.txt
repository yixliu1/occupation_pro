<h1>Occupation Name: Automotive Electrician</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 321111</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Automotive Electrician installs, maintains and repairs electrical wiring and electronic components in motor vehicles. Registration or licensing may be required.</p>
<p>Automotive electricians may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>work with computer-controlled engine management systems</li>
<li>service, identify and repair faults on electronically controlled vehicle systems such as electronic fuel injection, electronic ignition, anti-lock braking, cruise control, automatic transmission, airbags and air conditioning</li>
<li>install electrical equipment such as gauges, lighting, alternators and starter motors in vehicles</li>
<li>install electrically operated accessories such as radios, heating or demisting equipment, air conditioners, driving lamps and anti-theft systems</li>
<li>refer to circuit diagrams, and use meters and test instruments to find electrical faults</li>
<li>adjust engine control systems and timing to ensure vehicles are running at peak performance</li>
<li>test, recondition and replace faulty alternators, generators, starter motors and related items such as voltage regulators and batteries</li>
<li>repair or replace faulty ignition, electrical wiring, fuses, lamps and switches</li>
<li>use hand tools, specialised electrical tools, instruments and machines, including drills, grinders, presses and lathes</li>
<li>solder or weld when repairing electrical parts</li>
<li>sell and install electrical parts and accessories</li>
<li>install, repair and service air conditioning systems.</li>
