<h1>Occupation Name: Textile, Clothing and Footwear Mechanic</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 323215</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Textile, Clothing and Footwear Mechanic sets up, adjusts and maintains industrial or domestic sewing machines, or machines used in the production of yarn, textiles or footwear.</p>
<p>Textile mechanics may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>use tools and instruments to set up machines</li>
<li>start machines for trial runs to ensure they are running properly</li>
<li>diagnose and fix processing faults on textile equipment</li>
<li>inspect yarns and fabrics produced</li>
<li>repair or replace faulty parts</li>
<li>organise a shift to run smoothly and prevent problems from occurring</li>
<li>adjust machine settings to meet design specifications.</li>
