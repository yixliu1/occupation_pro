<h1>Occupation Name: Engraver</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 323311</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Engraver inscribes letters, figures and designs on metal, glass, wood, rubber, plastic and other surfaces.</p>
<p>Engravers may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>lay out lettering or designs on objects by sketching on paper or in powder on the objects to be engraved</li>
<li>mark outlines of designs using scribers, mount objects in vices and cut designs with engraving tools</li>
<li>clean and polish engraved areas</li>
<li>sketch original designs, or copy sketches or photographs</li>
<li>brush powder or solutions onto surfaces of dies or plates that imprint designs</li>
<li>use acid etching to produce photographic images onto metals</li>
<li>use pantographs to enlarge or reduce designs</li>
<li>operate engraving machines to cut standard designs.</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation is on the Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme Occupation List (RSMS ROL) , you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclass:</p>
<li>Regional Sponsor Migration Scheme (subclass 187)</li>
