<h1>Occupation Name: Gunsmith</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 323312</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Gunsmith modifies, services and repairs rifles, revolvers and other firearms. Registration or licensing is required.</p>
<p>Gunsmith may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>assembling parts and subassemblies of precision instruments, locks, timepieces and firearms</li>
<li>dismantling precision instruments, locks, timepieces and firearms, repairing and replacing defective parts, and reassembling articles using hand and power tools and specially designed machines</li>
<li>inscribing letters, figures and designs on surfaces of jewellery, trophies and other ornamental items</li>
<li>installing security systems, changing tumblers in locks, changing locks, cutting keys and opening locks by manipulation</li>
<li>calibrating precision instruments using standard weights and measures, jigs and fixtures, and hand tools to adjust and align parts and small balancing weights</li>
<li>making blades for circular, band and other power saws and repairing, setting and sharpening blades for hand and power saws</li>
<li>testing circuits in electronic timepieces</li>
<li>may estimate costs and prepare quotes for repairs</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
