<h1>Occupation Name: Locksmith</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 323313</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Locksmith installs and maintains locks and related security devices and systems. Registration or licensing is required.</p>
<p>Locksmiths may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>sell and install high-security lock systems, key control systems, all types of mechanical locks and keyless entry locks</li>
<li>repair, replace or adjust damaged or defective components of doors, including door closers, hinges and electronic release mechanisms</li>
<li>change mechanical and electronic key codes</li>
<li>design, manufacture, install and manage restricted master key systems</li>
<li>determine customer security requirements and assess risks through discussion and on-site inspection</li>
<li>assist clients to gain entry into premises, automotive vehicles, safes or high-security storage containers such as filing cabinets</li>
<li>recommend appropriate security and locking systems such as safes, strong rooms, alarms, closed-circuit television (CCTV) and door and window locks</li>
<li>install, replace and maintain locking systems, alarms and access control systems</li>
<li>test the performance of locks and security systems</li>
<li>repair jammed, worn or damaged locks by manipulating, dismantling, repairing or replacing defective parts</li>
<li>sell locks, safes and associated hardware</li>
<li>cut new or duplicate keys for locks and other security systems and devices</li>
<li>keep records of specifications of installed locking systems.</li>
