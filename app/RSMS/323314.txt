<h1>Occupation Name: Precision Instrument Maker and Repairer</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 323314</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Metal Fitters and Machinists (nec) assembles, calibrates, installs and overhauls mechanical precision instruments and equipment.</p>
<p>Precision Instrument Maker and Repairer may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>assembling parts and subassemblies of precision instruments, locks, timepieces and firearms</li>
<li>dismantling precision instruments, locks, timepieces and firearms, repairing and replacing defective parts, and reassembling articles using hand and power tools and specially designed machines</li>
<li>inscribing letters, figures and designs on surfaces of jewellery, trophies and other ornamental items</li>
<li>installing security systems, changing tumblers in locks, changing locks, cutting keys and opening locks by manipulation</li>
<li>calibrating precision instruments using standard weights and measures, jigs and fixtures, and hand tools to adjust and align parts and small balancing weights</li>
<li>making blades for circular, band and other power saws and repairing, setting and sharpening blades for hand and power saws</li>
<li>testing circuits in electronic timepieces</li>
<li>may estimate costs and prepare quotes for repairs</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation belongs to Short-term Skilled Occupation List (STSOL) . STSOL consists of the list of the occupations which are high in demand in Australia. If you want to apply for Australia sponsored Permanent Residency (subclass 190) your occupation must be listed in the relevant STSOL list and you must have an invitation/sponsorship from the state government. This list is also a part of the Australia Point system. If eligible, you can live and work in Australia for an indefinite period of time.</p>
<p>If your occupation is on STSOL, you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclasses:</p>
<li>Skilled Nominated visa (subclass 190)</li>
<li>Training visa (subclass 407)</li>
<li>Skilled Work Regional (Provisional) visa (subclass 491) - State or Territory nominated</li>
<li>Temporary Skill Shortage visa (subclass 482) - Short-term stream</li>
<li>Skilled Employer Sponsored Regional (Provisional) visa (Subclass 494)</li>
