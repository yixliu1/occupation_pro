<h1>Occupation Name: Saw Doctor ( Saw Maker and Repairer)</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 323315</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Saw Doctor ( Saw Maker and Repairer) makes, repairs, sets and sharpens blades for circular, band and other saws.</p>
<p>Saw maker and repairer may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>manufacture saw blades from blank sheets or strips of steel</li>
<li>straighten and stretch steel in circular and band saws</li>
<li>operate hand and power tools, machines and mechanical equipment</li>
<li>mill, grind, press or sharpen cutting tools</li>
<li>weld tools, saws and cutting edges</li>
<li>manufacture and maintain the straightness and balance in all cutting tools and saw blades</li>
<li>use hand skills to manipulate machines to sharpen saws and cutting tools so that they produce the required surface finish on sawn or milled products</li>
<li>maintain a range of mechanical parts on sawing machinery used in timber processing.</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation is on the Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme Occupation List (RSMS ROL) , you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclass:</p>
<li>Regional Sponsor Migration Scheme (subclass 187)</li>
