<h1>Occupation Name: Watch and Clock Maker and Repairer</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 323316</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Watch and Clock Maker and Repairer makes, repairs, cleans and adjusts watches and clocks.</p>
<p>Watch and clock repairers may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>replace watch power sources such as batteries and capacitors</li>
<li>perform minor repairs to watch parts including bands, glass, crown and winder</li>
<li>test watches for water resistance to agreed depths and Australian standards</li>
<li>remove watch and clock mechanisms from cases and examine them for signs of wear or damage</li>
<li>repair or replace faulty parts such as cracked crystals, broken hands and bent teeth or wheels</li>
<li>clean, test and lubricate watch and clock components</li>
<li>test and analyse electronic circuits in quartz watches and clocks</li>
<li>reassemble, fit and adjust timepieces to make sure they work correctly</li>
<li>provide customers with description and estimated cost of repairs</li>
<li>sell new and used watches and clocks to the public.</li>
