<h1>Occupation Name: Panelbeater</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 324111</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Panelbeater repairs damage to metal, fibreglass and plastic body work on vehicles and forms replacement vehicle panels. Registration or licensing may be required.</p>
<p>Panel beaters may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>remove damaged panels and components by unbolting or cutting them away</li>
<li>repair damaged body panels with specialist tools and equipment</li>
<li>use measuring, mechanical and hydraulic equipment to restore vehicles to factory clearances</li>
<li>cut and join replacement sections or damaged panels using correct welding procedures</li>
<li>repair dents with plastic fillers, and file, grind and sand repaired surfaces using power and hand tools</li>
<li>fit the repaired panels on the framework of the vehicle and refit body hardware, such as door locks and door trims</li>
<li>replace badly damaged sections with new or second-hand panels</li>
<li>realign chassis and body frames using chassis aligners and measuring systems</li>
<li>remove and replace accident-related mechanical items</li>
<li>remove and replace auto-electric components</li>
<li>help vehicle body builders to build custom-designed, speciality or vintage vehicles</li>
<li>assist with spray painting vehicles.</li>
