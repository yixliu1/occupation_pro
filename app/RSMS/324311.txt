<h1>Occupation Name: Vehicle Painter</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 324311</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Vehicle Painter prepares surfaces of vehicles, matches and mixes colours and applies paint. Registration or licensing may be required.</p>
<p>Vehicle painters may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>prepare surfaces to be painted by hand or machine</li>
<li>mix and match paint by eye or by using computer technology to ensure correct colour match</li>
<li>apply primer and finishing coats using spray guns and rub down surfaces between coats</li>
<li>touch up paintwork and apply polish to vehicles</li>
<li>remove masking papers before waxing and polishing finished paintwork</li>
<li>paint signs or artwork on vehicles</li>
<li>treat vehicles with rust-proofing chemicals.</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
