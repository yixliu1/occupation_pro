<h1>Occupation Name: Carpenter</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 331212</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Carpenter constructs, erects, installs, renovates and repairs structures and fixtures of wood, plywood, wallboard and other materials. Registration or licensing may be required.</p>
<p>Carpenters may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>construct formwork into which concrete is poured</li>
<li>set out an outline of the building on the ground of the site, using string and pegs to allow for excavations</li>
<li>build floors, wall frameworks (timber or metal) and roofs, and lay timber floors</li>
<li>read plans and specifications to determine the dimensions, materials required and installation processes to be followed</li>
<li>install metal and timber windows, sashes and doors</li>
<li>construct and erect prefabricated units, such as cottages and houses</li>
<li>cut materials with hand and power tools, and assemble, nail, cut or shape parts</li>
<li>install door handles, locks, hardware, flooring underlay, insulating material and other fixtures</li>
<li>maintain and sharpen tools.</li>
