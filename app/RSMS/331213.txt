<h1>Occupation Name: Joiner</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 331213</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Joiner cuts, shapes and fits timber parts in workshops to form structures and fittings, ready for installation. Registration or licensing may be required.</p>
<p>Joiners may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>determine job requirements from drawings, templates and written instructions</li>
<li>set up jigs, select timber and cut timber to size and shape using templates</li>
<li>cut joints using wood-cutting machines or hand tools</li>
<li>assemble prepared wood to form structures and fittings</li>
<li>work with plastic laminates, perspex or metals</li>
<li>maintain and sharpen tools.</li>
