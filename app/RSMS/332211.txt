<h1>Occupation Name: Painting Trades Worker</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 332211</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Painting Trades Worker applies paint, varnish, wallpaper and other finishes to protect, maintain and decorate surfaces of buildings and structures. Registration or licensing may be required.</p>
<p>Painting Trades Worker may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>erecting scaffolding and ladders, and placing drop sheets to protect adjacent areas from paint splattering</li>
<li>preparing surfaces by removing old paint and wallpaper, fixing woodwork, filling holes and cracks, and smoothing and sealing surfaces</li>
<li>selecting and preparing paints to required colours by mixing portions of pigment, oil, and thinning and drying additives</li>
<li>applying paints, varnishes and stains to surfaces using brushes, rollers and sprays</li>
<li>hanging wallpaper, matching patterns and trimming edges</li>
<li>cleaning equipment and work areas</li>
<li>may repair windows and replace glass in wooden and metal frames</li>
<li>may lay and repair wall and floor tiles</li>
