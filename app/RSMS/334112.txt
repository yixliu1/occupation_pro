<h1>Occupation Name: Airconditioning and Mechanical Services Plumber</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 334112</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Airconditioning and Mechanical Services Plumber installs, maintains and repairs piping, ducting and equipment for heating, cooling and ventilation of buildings or vessels. Registration or licensing is required.</p>
<p>Airconditioning and Mechanical Services Plumber may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>studying blueprints, drawings and specifications to determine the layout of plumbing systems and materials required</li>
<li>setting out and installing hot and cold water systems and associated equipment</li>
<li>installing water-based fire protections systems, including fire hydrants, hose reels and sprinkler systems</li>
<li>designing and installing sanitary plumbing and water supply systems, discharge pipes and sanitary fixtures</li>
<li>fabricating and installing soil and waste stacks</li>
<li>assembling and installing mechanical services plant, air handling and conditioning equipment and small bore heating systems</li>
<li>installing sewerage and effluent pumping equipment and disposal systems</li>
<li>installing below-ground drainage systems and associated ground support systems</li>
<li>installing gas appliances, flues and pressure regulating devices</li>
<li>fabricating and installing metal roofing, rainwater goods and flashings</li>
