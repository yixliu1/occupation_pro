<h1>Occupation Name: Drainer</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 334113</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Drainer installs, maintains and designs below-ground drainage systems and associated sewerage or effluent disposal systems. Registration or licensing is required.</p>
<p>Drainlayers may perform the following tasks: </p>
<li>set out and install below-ground sanitary and stormwater drainage systems</li>
<li>carry out site drainage and de-watering procedures, employing stormwater, sub-soil and pumped systems</li>
<li>install septic tanks, effluent disposal and disinfection systems</li>
<li>find and clear blockages in drainage systems</li>
<li>replace sections of below-ground drains</li>
<li>excavate and level according to line, depth and grade</li>
<li>install excavation supports in accordance with Occupational Health and Safety requirements</li>
<li>cut and join piping</li>
<li>test drainage systems</li>
<li>construct brick or concrete pits.</li>
