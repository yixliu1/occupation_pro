<h1>Occupation Name: Gasfitter</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 334114</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Gasfitter installs, maintains and repairs gas mains, piping systems downstream of the billing meter, and appliances and ancillary equipment associated with the use of fuel gases, including liquefied petroleum gas systems. Registration or licensing is required.</p>
<p>Gasfitters may perform the following tasks: </p>
<li>interpret drawings and specifications</li>
<li>measure and mark reference points for location of equipment</li>
<li>test piping systems for gas appliances</li>
<li>install piping systems for combustible gases</li>
<li>install gas appliances and ancillary equipment</li>
<li>install appliance flues</li>
<li>install gas pressure regulating devices and gas detection systems</li>
<li>provide consumer information about the operation and maintenance of appliances</li>
<li>install LPG systems in caravans and marine craft</li>
<li>undertake maintenance of gas appliances</li>
<li>maintain pipes, appliances, flues and ancillary equipment associated with the use of fuel gases.</li>
