<h1>Occupation Name: Electrician (Special Class)</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 341112</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Electrician (Special Class) services and repairs intricate and complex electrical and electronic circuitry. Registration or licensing is required.</p>
<p>Electrician (Special Class) may perform the following tasks: </p>
<li>examining blueprints, wiring diagrams and specifications to determine sequences and methods of operation</li>
<li>measuring and laying out installation reference points</li>
<li>selecting, cutting and connecting wire and cable to terminals and connectors</li>
<li>using electrical and electronic test instruments to trace and diagnose faults</li>
<li>repairing and replacing faulty wiring and defective parts</li>
<li>positioning and installing electrical switchboards</li>
<li>connecting electrical systems to power supply</li>
<li>testing continuity of circuit</li>
<li>installing, testing and adjusting electric and mechanical parts of lifts</li>
