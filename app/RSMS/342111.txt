<h1>Occupation Name: Airconditioning and Refrigeration Mechanic</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 342111</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Airconditioning and Refrigeration Mechanic assembles, installs, maintains and repairs industrial, commercial and domestic airconditioning and refrigeration systems and equipment. Registration or licensing may be required.</p>
<p>Airconditioning and Refrigeration Mechanic may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>establishing job requirements from drawings and specifications, and laying out installation reference points</li>
<li>drilling holes, installing mounting brackets and cutting, bending and threading piping</li>
<li>installing and repairing components such as compressors, motors, condensers, evaporators, switches and gauges, and copper lines for steam, gas, refrigerant, compressed air, oil and chilled water</li>
<li>bolting, soldering, riveting, welding and brazing pipes to connect equipment, and checking alignment and accuracy of fit</li>
<li>filling systems with gas or fluid to check for leaks</li>
<li>test-operating refrigeration systems, checking mechanisms and making adjustments</li>
<li>removing test gas and fluid using vacuum pumps, and filling with refrigerant</li>
<li>checking and overhauling refrigeration systems, diagnosing faults and repairing and replacing defective components</li>
<li>adjusting system controls and mechanisms and reassembling systems</li>
<li>recording causes of malfunctioning and action taken</li>
