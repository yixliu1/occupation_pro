<h1>Occupation Name: Telecommunications Cable Jointer</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 342412</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Telecommunications Cable Jointer joints, terminates and repairs copper and fibre optic telecommunications cables installed in underground pipes, trenches and overhead systems.</p>
<p>Data and telecommunications cable workers may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>install, join or splice metallic and fibre-optic cables</li>
<li>perform relevant cable tests to ensure joints and fibre splices comply with site and manufacturer specifications and relevant legislation</li>
<li>install underground and aerial lead-in cables</li>
<li>install above-ground equipment enclosures</li>
<li>fix any cable faults</li>
<li>perform fault clearance</li>
<li>tag cables for future identification.</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation is on the Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme Occupation List (RSMS ROL) , you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclass:</p>
<li>Regional Sponsor Migration Scheme (subclass 187)</li>
