<h1>Occupation Name: Telecommunications Technician</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 342414</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Telecommunications Technician installs, maintains and repairs telecommunications equipment and appliances, such as telephones, mobile telephones, switchboards and data transmission equipment, in homes, businesses, telephone exchanges and other network sites.</p>
<p>Telecommunications technicians may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>install fixed and wireless network equipment and set up new telecommunications services</li>
<li>ensure that equipment and circuit installations are of good quality</li>
<li>place or terminate cables, including those made of optical fibre</li>
<li>undertake proof tests such as line testing, circuit commissioning and power tests</li>
<li>assemble, erect, position and label items of equipment</li>
<li>provide cost estimates to customers for installation of equipment</li>
<li>install and maintain telecommunication and other data systems at customers' premises</li>
<li>install, test and maintain all types of telecommunications, switching and transmission equipment</li>
<li>set up computer hardware and install and customise software applications used in telecommunications</li>
<li>carry out modifications to items of equipment</li>
<li>analyse system faults with a high degree of diagnostic skill (to circuit board level)</li>
<li>maintain and adhere to operational procedures and complete appropriate documentation</li>
<li>take responsibility for tools, and plant and test equipment</li>
<li>develop and maintain good relations with internal and external customers</li>
<li>operate call tracing facilities when necessary.</li>
