<h1>Occupation Name: Butcher or Smallgoods Maker</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 351211</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Butcher or Smallgoods Maker selects, cuts, trims, prepares and arranges meat for sale or supply, operates meat or smallgoods processing machines, or manages the processes in the production of smallgoods.</p>
<p>Butchers may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>cut, trim and shape standard cuts of meat to size</li>
<li>process carcasses into primary and secondary cuts</li>
<li>pack and store products for display and sale</li>
<li>order supplies from wholesalers using AUS-MEAT Language, and weigh and check stock on receipt</li>
<li>assemble and operate mincers, grinders, mixers, patty-forming or sausage-forming machines, and bandsaws</li>
<li>use correct detergents and sanitisers to prevent food poisoning and contamination of premises and equipment</li>
<li>assist clients (including hotels and restaurants) with menu planning, estimating food portions and production costs</li>
<li>collect payment for sales</li>
<li>prepare marinated meats and ready-to-cook dishes</li>
<li>advise customers about alternative cuts, cooking methods, storage requirements and nutritional aspects of meat.</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation belongs to Short-term Skilled Occupation List (STSOL) . STSOL consists of the list of the occupations which are high in demand in Australia. If you want to apply for Australia sponsored Permanent Residency (subclass 190) your occupation must be listed in the relevant STSOL list and you must have an invitation/sponsorship from the state government. This list is also a part of the Australia Point system. If eligible, you can live and work in Australia for an indefinite period of time.</p>
<p>If your occupation is on STSOL, you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclasses:</p>
<li>Skilled Nominated visa (subclass 190)</li>
<li>Training visa (subclass 407)</li>
<li>Skilled Work Regional (Provisional) visa (subclass 491) - State or Territory nominated</li>
<li>Temporary Skill Shortage visa (subclass 482) - Short-term stream</li>
<li>Skilled Employer Sponsored Regional (Provisional) visa (Subclass 494)</li>
