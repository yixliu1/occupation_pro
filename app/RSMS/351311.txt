<h1>Occupation Name: Chef</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 351311</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Chef plans and organises the preparation and cooking of food in a dining or catering establishment.</p>
<p>Chefs may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>plan menus and determine food and labour costs</li>
<li>recruit and train staff</li>
<li>plan staff rosters and supervise the activities of cooks and assistants</li>
<li>discuss food preparation issues with managers, dietitians and other staff members</li>
<li>order food, kitchen supplies and equipment</li>
<li>demonstrate techniques to cooks and advise on cooking procedures</li>
<li>prepare and cook food</li>
<li>explain and enforce hygiene regulations</li>
<li>freeze and preserve foods.</li>
