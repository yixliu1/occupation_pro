<h1>Occupation Name: Horse Trainer</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 361112</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Horse Trainer prepares horses for riding, breeding, racing, work, show or competitions. Registration or licensing may be required.</p>
<p>Horse trainers may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>carry out practical daily tasks, such as saddling, bridling, feeding and grooming horses</li>
<li>accustom horses to racing equipment such as reins and harnesses, hitches and sulkies (two-wheeled carriages)</li>
<li>teach apprentice jockeys, drivers and riders horse riding techniques and horse handling methods</li>
<li>supervise and direct stable staff, jockeys, sulky drivers and other workers</li>
<li>plan, supervise and carry out training programmes for horses</li>
<li>plan and select race programmes to achieve the best placing for a horse</li>
<li>plan, supervise and carry out nutritional programmes for horses</li>
<li>monitor the health of the horses and consult veterinarians on injuries and ailments</li>
<li>advise and consult with horse owners</li>
<li>attend race meetings or other horse-related events</li>
<li>keep accurate records of accounts and use correct credit procedures.</li>
