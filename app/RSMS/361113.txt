<h1>Occupation Name: Pet Groomer</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 361113</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: VETASSESS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Pet Groomer washes, dries, brushes, combs, cuts and styles pets' coats, clips their nails and cleans their ears.</p>
<p>Pet Groomer may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>teaching animals to obey verbal and non-verbal commands and addressing behavioural problems</li>
<li>training animals to accept riders and pull vehicles</li>
<li>training animals to perform in competitions</li>
<li>bathing, cutting, combing, blow-drying and styling pets' coats, clipping their nails and cleaning their ears</li>
<li>inspecting, preparing, cleaning, disinfecting and maintaining comfortable animal cages and enclosures</li>
<li>transporting food, filling water troughs and feeding animals according to their individual needs</li>
<li>maintaining animal health records, treating minor injuries and reporting serious conditions to Veterinarians</li>
<li>exercising and playing with animals, answering visitor questions, and transferring animals between enclosures by leading or carrying them</li>
<li>dusting and spraying insecticides on animals and immersing them in insecticide baths, to control insect pests</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation is on the Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme Occupation List (RSMS ROL) , you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclass:</p>
<li>Regional Sponsor Migration Scheme (subclass 187)</li>
<li>Skilled Employer Sponsored Regional (Provisional) visa (Subclass 494)</li>
<h2>Requirements for Skills Assessment</h2>
<p>Pet Groomer is classified as Group F occupations by Vetassess. The criteria for skills assessment of Group F occupations are the following:</p>
<li>Qualification/s assessed at least AQF Certificate II with a highly relevant field of study and at least one year of postqualification highly relevant employment at an appropriate skill level in the last five years;</li>
<li>Qualification/s assessed at Australian Certificate II without a highly relevant field of study and at least two years of post-qualification highly relevant employment at an appropriate skill level in the last five years</li>
<p>According to Vetassess, if you have pre-qualification employment and if all of your employment is before your qualifications assessed at the AQF Certificate II, three years of highly relevant employment in addition to at least one year of highly relevant employment at an appropriate skill level completed in the last five years.</p>
