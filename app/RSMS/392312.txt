<h1>Occupation Name: Small Offset Printer</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 392312</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Small Offset Printer sets up and operates small offset printing presses used in instant print shops or for in-house printing.</p>
<p>Small Offset Printer may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>setting, adjusting and monitoring substrate-feed mechanisms, delivery mechanisms, inking systems and other printing machine functions</li>
<li>mixing ink and solvents to standard, and regulating paper and ink supply during print runs</li>
<li>monitoring, evaluating and determining press operations manually and by computer to check print quality standards against proofs and detect malfunctions</li>
<li>producing a variety of printed products using relief, lithographic, flexographic and gravure printing presses, and in-line finishing systems</li>
<li>preparing plates, blankets and impression cylinders on small offset lithographic printing presses</li>
<li>loading paper into feeding mechanisms</li>
<li>monitoring machine operations and quality of printing</li>
<li>undertaking maintenance, adjustment, repair and cleaning of machines</li>
<li>producing and managing digital print images, and transferring and outputting images</li>
<li>may set up and operate paper and bookbinding guillotines</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
