<h1>Occupation Name: Canvas Goods Fabricator</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 393111</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Canvas Goods Fabricator fabricates and repairs canvas and related products such as awnings, tents, tarpaulins, horse rugs and caravan annexes.</p>
<p>Canvas Goods Fabricator may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>cutting and preparing canvas, leather and sailcloth to design specifications, patterns and drawings</li>
<li>sewing, gluing and riveting sections of canvas together to make articles such as awnings, tents, tarpaulins and horse rugs</li>
<li>attaching grommets, fastenings and other fittings to canvas goods</li>
<li>joining parts of leather articles using rivets, hand sewing, sewing machines, tools and adhesive</li>
<li>restoring and repairing leather articles</li>
<li>fabricating sails</li>
<li>designing patterns and prototypes of boots and shoes</li>
<li>making and grading patterns using manual and computerised methods</li>
<li>clicking synthetics, corrected grains, leather linings and leather outers by hand and machine</li>
<li>altering and repairing footwear</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation is on the Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme Occupation List (RSMS ROL) , you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclass:</p>
<li>Regional Sponsor Migration Scheme (subclass 187)</li>
