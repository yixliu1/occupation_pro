<h1>Occupation Name: Leather Goods Maker</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 393112</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Leather Goods Maker fabricates and repairs leather articles such as wallets, cases, harnesses and saddlery.</p>
<p>Leather goods makers may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>select suitable types and grades of leather</li>
<li>draw patterns</li>
<li>cut leather to size and shape using special cutting machines called 'clicking' and 'skiving' machines</li>
<li>join pieces together with rivets, threads or adhesives</li>
<li>sew by hand using needles</li>
<li>fill saddles and collars with hair or flock (stuffing)</li>
<li>attach buckles, eyelets and metal decorations, and emboss decorative patterns</li>
<li>finish products with stain, dye, polish or coats of lacquer</li>
<li>repair damaged goods.</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation is on the Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme Occupation List (RSMS ROL) , you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclass:</p>
<li>Regional Sponsor Migration Scheme (subclass 187)</li>
