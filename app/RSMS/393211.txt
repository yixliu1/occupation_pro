<h1>Occupation Name: Apparel Cutter</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 393211</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Apparel Cutter lays out, marks and cuts fabric to form parts of garments.</p>
<p>Apparel Cutter may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>conferring with customers to determine material, styles and designs of garments</li>
<li>interpreting designs, sketches and samples to determine pattern specifications</li>
<li>cutting out master patterns</li>
<li>laying up and cutting fabric</li>
<li>pinning, basting and draping garment parts</li>
<li>sewing garments</li>
<li>fitting basted garments on customers and marking areas requiring alteration</li>
<li>sewing buttonholes, and sewing on buttons, hooks, eyes and press fasteners to finish garments</li>
<li>pressing and finishing work</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation is on the Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme Occupation List (RSMS ROL) , you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclass:</p>
<li>Regional Sponsor Migration Scheme (subclass 187)</li>
