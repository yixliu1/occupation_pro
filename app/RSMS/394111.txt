<h1>Occupation Name: Cabinetmaker</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 394111</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Cabinetmaker fabricates or repairs wooden furniture, and fits and assembles prepared wooden parts to make furniture.</p>
<p>Cabinetmakers may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>communicate with clients to determine specific job requirements</li>
<li>match furnishing styles and materials to clients' needs</li>
<li>deliver and monitor service to clients</li>
<li>estimate job costs</li>
<li>measure and draw site layouts for manufactured furniture</li>
<li>read and interpret work documents in order to prepare accurate cutting lists for jobs</li>
<li>programme and control computer-aided design (CAD) and computer numerical control (CNC) machines</li>
<li>understand and select timber and timber products for furniture production</li>
<li>fabricate custom furniture</li>
<li>prepare and assemble cabinets for installation</li>
<li>select and install hardware to furnishings, such as handles and hinges</li>
<li>perform on-site repairs and adjustments of cabinets</li>
<li>clean and maintain work areas, including machinery and tools, to ensure a safe working environment.</li>
