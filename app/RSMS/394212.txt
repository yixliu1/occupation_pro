<h1>Occupation Name: Picture Framer</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 394212</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Picture Framer cuts out and assembles mouldings to make picture frames, and frames paintings, photographs, needlework and other artwork.</p>
<p>Picture framers may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>talk to customers about the style, size, materials and cost of their framing requirements</li>
<li>help customers to understand the colours, design and layout of frames</li>
<li>assess the value and physical condition of artwork to decide the level of framing required</li>
<li>order the materials needed and maintain accounts</li>
<li>cut frame pieces to size and prepare joints for corners</li>
<li>join the frame with nails, glue or dowels and sand lightly</li>
<li>mould and glue ornamental parts into the frame if required</li>
<li>varnish, stain or paint the frame</li>
<li>mount backing materials and the item for framing</li>
<li>cut glass to size</li>
<li>fit items to ready-made frames</li>
<li>refer to specialists such as conservators to assess the physical condition of artwork</li>
<li>assist in the preservation of historic artwork.</li>
