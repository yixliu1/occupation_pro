<h1>Occupation Name: Boat Builder and Repairer</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 399111</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Boat Builder and Repairer builds, repairs and modifies boats. Registration or licensing is required.</p>
<p>Boat Builder and Repairer may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>studying plans and specifications, and preparing templates and scale plans for fabrication and cutting of hull sections</li>
<li>marking reference points and lines on dry docks and slipways</li>
<li>checking position and functioning of slipway apparatus</li>
<li>assembling shells of boats and erecting hull sections of ship</li>
<li>erecting and preparing launching platforms, conducting pre-launch tests and supervising launching procedures</li>
<li>installing masts, frames, decking, fittings, machines, shafts and safety equipment</li>
<li>building and installing structures such as cabins, machine mountings, propeller supports and rudders</li>
<li>determining repair requirements and procedures</li>
<li>may make hull moulds and fabricate and repair vessels using materials such as aluminium, wood, glass, reinforced plastics, carbon fibre, Kevlar, fibreglass and concrete</li>
