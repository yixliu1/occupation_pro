<h1>Occupation Name: Shipwright</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 399112</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Shipwright constructs, fits out and repairs ships. Registration or licensing is required.</p>
<p>Shipwrights may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>prepare preliminary sketches of structural parts and sections of vessels</li>
<li>produce templates and patterns from full-size detail drawings</li>
<li>fit out internal and external components of vessels</li>
<li>position sections accurately prior to permanent fastening</li>
<li>prepare slipways and construct cradles used to support ships during construction, transportation, launching and slipping</li>
<li>supervise launching and slipping procedures</li>
<li>carry out repairs.</li>
