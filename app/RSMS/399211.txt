<h1>Occupation Name: Chemical Plant Operator</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 399211</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Chemical Plant Operator controls the operation of chemical production plant.</p>
<p>Chemical plant operators may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>prepare and measure raw materials</li>
<li>feed raw materials and processing agents into plant machinery</li>
<li>set controls and operate machinery</li>
<li>check instruments and equipment to ensure correct operation, and attend to any abnormal operating conditions</li>
<li>take samples for testing, test products and record process data</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation is on Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme Occupation List (RSMS ROL) , you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclass:</p>
<li>Regional Sponsor Migration Scheme (subclass 187)</li>
