<h1>Occupation Name: Gallery or Museum Technician</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 399311</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Gallery or Museum Technician repares artworks, specimens and artefacts for collections, and arranges and constructs gallery or museum exhibits.</p>
<p>Gallery or Museum Technician may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>mounting and preparing objects for display</li>
<li>designing and arranging exhibit furnishings, display cases and display areas</li>
<li>assisting in setting up lighting and display equipment</li>
<li>receiving, shipping, packing and unpacking exhibits</li>
<li>ordering new library materials and maintaining library records and circulation systems</li>
<li>cataloguing printed and recorded material</li>
<li>entering data into databases and editing computer records</li>
<li>operating audiovisual and reprographic equipment</li>
<li>searching and verifying bibliographic data</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation is on the Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme Occupation List (RSMS ROL) , you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclass:</p>
<li>Regional Sponsor Migration Scheme (subclass 187)</li>
