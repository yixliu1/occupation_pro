<h1>Occupation Name: Jeweller</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 399411</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Jeweller makes and repairs jewellery such as rings, brooches, chains and bracelets, crafts objects out of precious metals, or cuts, shapes and polishes rough gemstones to produce fashion or industrial jewels.</p>
<p>Jewellers may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>create design drawings and specifications for wearable or three-dimensional objects</li>
<li>shape metal and other materials by cutting, filing, hammering, turning, spinning, bending, casting, folding and linking using specialised hand and power tools and equipment</li>
<li>assemble articles using soldering, screwing, riveting and other joining methods</li>
<li>finish articles using files, emery paper, buffing machines or other appropriate tools and equipment</li>
<li>secure gemstones in settings</li>
<li>engrave designs on ring settings, brooches, bracelets and other articles</li>
<li>repair jewellery by soldering, replacing or rebuilding worn or broken parts</li>
<li>remodel old jewellery</li>
<li>sell jewellery direct to the public or to retail jewellery shops.</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation belongs to Short-term Skilled Occupation List (STSOL) . STSOL consists of the list of the occupations which are high in demand in Australia. If you want to apply for Australia sponsored Permanent Residency (subclass 190) your occupation must be listed in the relevant STSOL list and you must have an invitation/sponsorship from the state government. This list is also a part of the Australia Point system. If eligible, you can live and work in Australia for an indefinite period of time.</p>
<p>If your occupation is on STSOL, you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclasses:</p>
<li>Skilled Nominated visa (subclass 190)</li>
<li>Training visa (subclass 407)</li>
<li>Skilled Work Regional (Provisional) visa (subclass 491) - State or Territory nominated</li>
<li>Temporary Skill Shortage visa (subclass 482) - Short-term stream</li>
<li>Skilled Employer Sponsored Regional (Provisional) visa (Subclass 494)</li>
