<h1>Occupation Name: Broadcast Transmitter Operator</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 399511</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Broadcast Transmitter Operator operates consoles to control radio or television broadcast transmitters.</p>
<p>Broadcasting technicians may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>check equipment to ensure it is operating properly (antennas, remote controls and auxiliary power units, for example)</li>
<li>study reports and talk to equipment operators to get details of problems</li>
<li>replace faulty wiring and terminals, and re-solder connections</li>
<li>find and replace faulty assemblies and parts (such as capacitors, transistors, integrated circuits, printed circuit boards and transformers) using electronic test equipment</li>
<li>check output power, frequency and modulation using test equipment</li>
<li>assist with building, installing and adjusting new equipment.</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation is on the Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme Occupation List (RSMS ROL) , you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclass:</p>
<li>Regional Sponsor Migration Scheme (subclass 187)</li>
