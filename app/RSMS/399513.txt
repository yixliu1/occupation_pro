<h1>Occupation Name: Light Technician</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 399513</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Light Technician positions and controls lighting equipment for film, television or video productions or stage performances.</p>
<p>Light Technician may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>study scripts and discuss lighting effects with production staff</li>
<li>coordinate activities with electrical crew when putting up metal scaffolding to support lighting equipment</li>
<li>select and position equipment such as spotlights and floodlights</li>
<li>arrange and connect cables to electrical supplies using portable junction boxes</li>
<li>operate lights during filming or broadcasting and vary intensity of light using various filters and reflective screens</li>
<li>maintain and repair lighting equipment</li>
<li>arrange for transportation of equipment to remote locations.</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation is on the Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme Occupation List (RSMS ROL) , you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclass:</p>
<li>Regional Sponsor Migration Scheme (subclass 187)</li>
