<h1>Occupation Name: Musical Instrument Maker or Repairer</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 399515</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Musical Instrument Maker or Repairer builds, repairs and restores musical instruments, and modifies and tunes them to owners' specifications.</p>
<p>Musical Instrument Maker or Repairer may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>Playing instruments to evaluate their sound quality and to locate any defects.</li>
<li>Comparing instrument pitches with tuning tool pitches in order to tune instruments.</li>
<li>Disassembling instruments and parts for repair and adjustment.</li>
<li>Inspecting instruments to locate defects, and to determine their value or the level of restoration required.</li>
<li>Reassembling instruments following repair, using hand tools and power tools and glue, hair, yarn, resin, or clamps, and lubricate instruments as necessary.</li>
<li>Repairing or replacing musical instrument parts and components, such as strings, bridges, felts, and keys, using hand and power tools.</li>
<li>Shaping old parts and replacement parts to improve tone or intonation, using hand tools, lathes, or soldering irons.</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation is on the Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme Occupation List (RSMS ROL) , you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclass:</p>
<li>Regional Sponsor Migration Scheme (subclass 187)</li>
