<h1>Occupation Name: Optical Dispenser</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 399913</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Optical Dispenser interprets optical prescriptions, and fits and services optical appliances such as spectacle frames and lenses. Registration or licensing may be required.</p>
<p>Optical dispensers may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>discuss the client's eyewear needs</li>
<li>choose the appropriate frame to satisfy the client's comfort, appearance and prescription requirements</li>
<li>discuss and recommend types of lenses and lens enhancements</li>
<li>make sure that lenses conform to the prescription of the ophthalmologist (eye specialist) or optometrist</li>
<li>shape lenses and fit them to frames, making sure that they meet quality standards</li>
<li>make sure that the new eyewear fits correctly and comfortably</li>
<li>carry out minor repairs to damaged frames.</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation is on the Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme Occupation List (RSMS ROL) , you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclass:</p>
<li>Regional Sponsor Migration Scheme (subclass 187)</li>
