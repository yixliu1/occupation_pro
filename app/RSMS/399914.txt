<h1>Occupation Name: Optical Mechanic</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 399914</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: TRA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Optical Mechanic operates machines to grind, polish and surface optical lenses to meet prescription requirements, and fits lenses to spectacle frames.</p>
<p>Optical mechanics may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>read and interpret prescriptions from eye specialists</li>
<li>locate and mark optical centres and axes on lens blanks and make calculations to grind lenses to specifications</li>
<li>grind and polish the surface of the lens by setting grinding and polishing machines</li>
<li>test polished lenses for accuracy</li>
<li>apply protective coatings and non-reflective material to lenses and apply heat and chemical treatment to glass lenses to harden them if required</li>
<li>shape and fit lenses to spectacle frames</li>
<li>repair damaged and broken spectacles</li>
<li>cut and polish contact lenses to prescription requirements.</li>
