<h1>Occupation Name: Photographer's Assistant</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 399915</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: VETASSESS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Photographer's Assistant assists Photographers in taking and developing photographs.</p>
<p>Photographer's Assistant may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>Carry extra equipment for the photographer</li>
<li>Communicate with clients to truly understand their needs/desires</li>
<li>Perform administrative jobs such as billing, keeping records, contacting clients</li>
<li>Prep areas for photo shoots, disassemble these areas when finished</li>
<li>Process images and prepare them for final editing by the photographer</li>
<li>Set up lighting for shoots/single photographs</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation is on the Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme Occupation List (RSMS ROL) , you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclass:</p>
<li>Regional Sponsor Migration Scheme (subclass 187)</li>
<li>Skilled Employer Sponsored Regional (Provisional) visa (Subclass 494)</li>
<h2>Requirements for Skills Assessment</h2>
<p>Photographer's Assistant is classified as Group F occupations by Vetassess. The criteria for skills assessment of Group F occupations are the following:</p>
<li>Qualification/s assessed at least AQF Certificate II with a highly relevant field of study and at least one year of postqualification highly relevant employment at an appropriate skill level in the last five years;</li>
<li>Qualification/s assessed at Australian Certificate II without a highly relevant field of study and at least two years of post-qualification highly relevant employment at an appropriate skill level in the last five years</li>
<p>According to Vetassess, if you have pre-qualification employment and if all of your employment is before your qualifications assessed at the AQF Certificate II, three years of highly relevant employment in addition to at least one year of highly relevant employment at an appropriate skill level completed in the last five years.</p>
