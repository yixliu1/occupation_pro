<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>This occupation group covers Technicians and Trades Workers not elsewhere classified. Occupations in this group include:Airborne Electronics Analyst (Air Force), Architectural Model Maker, Canoe Maker, Fire Alarm Technician, Fire Extinguisher Technician, Glass Blower, Hide and Skin Classer, Kayak Maker, Micrographic Technician, Milking Machine Technician, Parachute Rigger, Pearl Technician, Pyrotechnician, Surfboard Maker,</p>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation is on the Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme Occupation List (RSMS ROL) , you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclass:</p>
<li>Regional Sponsor Migration Scheme (subclass 187)</li>
