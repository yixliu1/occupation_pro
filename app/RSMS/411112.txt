<h1>Occupation Name: Intensive Care Ambulance Paramedic</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 411112</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: VETASSESS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Intensive Care Ambulance Paramedic provides intensive pre-hospital health care to injured, sick, infirm and aged persons and emergency transport to medical facilities. Registration or licensing is required.</p>
<p>Intensive Care Ambulance Paramedic may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>attending accidents, emergencies and requests for medical assistance</li>
<li>assessing health of patients, determining need for assistance, and assessing specialised needs and factors affecting patients' conditions</li>
<li>performing therapies and administering drugs according to protocol</li>
<li>resuscitating and defibrillating patients and operating life-support equipment</li>
<li>transporting accident victims to medical facilities</li>
<li>transporting sick and disabled persons to and from medical facilities for specialised treatment and rehabilitation</li>
<li>instructing community groups and essential service workers in first aid</li>
<li>attending public gatherings and sporting events where accidents and other health emergencies may occur</li>
<li>ensuring that ambulances are adequately maintained and stocked with medical supplies, and that equipment is in good working order</li>
<li>preparing written reports on the state of patients' injuries and treatment provided</li>
