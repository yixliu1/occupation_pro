<h1>Occupation Name: Enrolled Nurse</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 411411</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: ANMAC</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Enrolled Nurse provides nursing care to patients in a variety of health, aged care, welfare and community settings under the supervision of Registered Nurses. Registration or licensing is required.</p>
<p>Enrolled nurses may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>assist with initial and ongoing health assessments and the maintenance of patients' health</li>
<li>observe, measure and record patients' conditions (their temperature, pulse, respiration, blood pressure and blood sugar levels, for example)</li>
<li>report changes in patients' conditions</li>
<li>attend to patients' hygiene and comfort by assisting with bathing, showering, dressing and bed-making</li>
<li>assist patients with meals when required</li>
<li>assist patients with rehabilitation exercises and programmes</li>
<li>assist in the care of wounds</li>
<li>assist in emergency situations</li>
<li>assist in providing education on health and lifestyle issues</li>
<li>maintain a safe environment for patients and assist with the prevention of injuries.</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation belongs to Short-term Skilled Occupation List (STSOL) . STSOL consists of the list of the occupations which are high in demand in Australia. If you want to apply for Australia sponsored Permanent Residency (subclass 190) your occupation must be listed in the relevant STSOL list and you must have an invitation/sponsorship from the state government. This list is also a part of the Australia Point system. If eligible, you can live and work in Australia for an indefinite period of time.</p>
<p>If your occupation is on STSOL, you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclasses:</p>
<li>Skilled Nominated visa (subclass 190)</li>
<li>Training visa (subclass 407)</li>
<li>Skilled Work Regional (Provisional) visa (subclass 491) - State or Territory nominated</li>
<li>Temporary Skill Shortage visa (subclass 482) - Short-term stream</li>
<li>Skilled Employer Sponsored Regional (Provisional) visa (Subclass 494)</li>
