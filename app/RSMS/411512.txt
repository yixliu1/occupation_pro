<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Kaiawhina (Hauora) (Maori Health Assistant) Assists with health care delivery to patients and clients in accordance with Tikanga Maori (Maori culture and custom).</p>
<p>Kaiawhina (Hauora) (Maori Health Assistant) may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>maintaining health records and statistics</li>
<li>acting as an advocate in the community they serve, and as a communicator and interpreter on behalf of clients and other health workers</li>
<li>providing clinical functions, such as case management and follow-up, independently or in consultation with other health care providers</li>
<li>providing health education to individual clients and staff in health facilities</li>
<li>providing cultural education to persons outside the cultural community and life skills education to the community they serve</li>
<li>providing counselling and referring clients to other health care providers where necessary</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>

<li>Regional Sponsor Migration Scheme (subclass 187)</li>
