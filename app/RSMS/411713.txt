<h1>Occupation Name: Family Support Worker</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 411713</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: ACWA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Family Support Worker assists the work of Social Workers and Welfare Workers by providing services and support to families.</p>
<p>Family Support Worker may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>assessing clients' needs and planning, developing and implementing educational, training and support programs</li>
<li>interviewing clients and assessing the nature and extent of difficulties</li>
<li>monitoring and reporting on the progress of clients</li>
<li>referring clients to agencies that can provide additional help</li>
<li>assessing community need and resources for health, welfare, housing, employment, training and other facilities and services</li>
<li>liaising with community groups, welfare agencies, government bodies and private businesses about community issues and promoting awareness of community resources and services</li>
<li>supporting families and providing education and care for children and disabled persons in adult service units, group housing and government institutions</li>
<li>supervising offenders on probation and parole</li>
<li>assisting young people to solve social, emotional and financial problems</li>
<li>preparing submissions for funding and resources, and reports to government bodies and other agencies</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation belongs to Short-term Skilled Occupation List (STSOL) . STSOL consists of the list of the occupations which are high in demand in Australia. If you want to apply for Australia sponsored Permanent Residency (subclass 190) your occupation must be listed in the relevant STSOL list and you must have an invitation/sponsorship from the state government. This list is also a part of the Australia Point system. If eligible, you can live and work in Australia for an indefinite period of time.</p>
<p>If your occupation is on STSOL, you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclasses:</p>
<li>Skilled Nominated visa (subclass 190)</li>
<li>Training visa (subclass 407)</li>
<li>Skilled Work Regional (Provisional) visa (subclass 491) - State or Territory nominated</li>
<li>Temporary Skill Shortage visa (subclass 482) - Short-term stream</li>
<li>Skilled Employer Sponsored Regional (Provisional) visa (Subclass 494)</li>
