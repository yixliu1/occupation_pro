<h1>Occupation Name: Child Care Worker</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 421111</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: ACECQA</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Child Care Worker provides care and supervision for children in programs, such as long day care and occasional care, in childcare centres, hospitals and educational centres. Registration or licensing may be required.</p>
<p>Child Care Worker may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>assisting in the preparation of materials and equipment for children's education and recreational activities</li>
<li>managing children's behaviour and guiding children's social development</li>
<li>preparing and conducting activities for children</li>
<li>entertaining children by reading and playing games</li>
<li>supervising children in recreational activities</li>
<li>supervising the daily routine of children</li>
<li>supervising the hygiene of children</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation is on the Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme Occupation List (RSMS ROL) , you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclass:</p>
<li>Regional Sponsor Migration Scheme (subclass 187)</li>
