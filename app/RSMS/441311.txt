<h1>Occupation Name: Detective</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 441311</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: VETASSESS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Detective investigates serious crimes, such as terrorism, homicide, armed robbery, vice and arson, and gathers evidence to arrest and prosecute suspected offenders.</p>
<p>Detective may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>investigating and prosecuting offences committed in areas such as organised, corporate and computer crime, environmental offences, drug trafficking, fraud, counterfeiting and terrorism</li>
<li>securing and examining scenes of crimes and accidents to locate and obtain evidence for analysis</li>
<li>protecting witnesses and investigating official corruption</li>
<li>maintaining public order and safety</li>
<li>patrolling assigned areas to minimise potential for public disturbance and crime</li>
<li>investigating accidents, crimes, minor offences and citizens' complaints, gathering evidence, and pursuing, arresting and interviewing suspected offenders</li>
<li>testing persons suspected of driving under the influence of alcohol and drugs and issuing infringement notices for traffic offences</li>
<li>directing and re-routing traffic at congested areas</li>
<li>attending community meetings and answering inquiries from the public where necessary</li>
<li>providing advice and assistance to victims of crime and their families</li>
<li>maintaining records and preparing reports</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation is on the Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme Occupation List (RSMS ROL) , you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclass:</p>
<li>Regional Sponsor Migration Scheme (subclass 187)</li>
