<h1>Occupation Name: Security Consultant</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 442216</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: VETASSESS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Security Consultant advises clients on security requirements, and recommends and designs security specifications. Registration or licensing may be required.</p>
<p>Security advisers may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>carry out threat assessments on properties or companies</li>
<li>assess existing security systems</li>
<li>make recommendations to improve security</li>
<li>design security systems to meet requirements</li>
<li>check security installations to make sure they comply with regulations</li>
<li>carry out electronic debugging or information security operations</li>
<li>carry out internal audits or other security functions within organisations</li>
<li>evaluate security system tenders.</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation is on the Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme Occupation List (RSMS ROL) , you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclass:</p>
<li>Regional Sponsor Migration Scheme (subclass 187)</li>
<li>Skilled Employer Sponsored Regional (Provisional) visa (Subclass 494)</li>
<h2>Requirements for Skills Assessment</h2>
<p>Security Consultant is classified as Group D occupations by Vetassess. The criteria for skills assessment of Group D occupations are the following:</p>
<li>Qualification(s) assessed at AQF Certificate IV with a highly relevant field of study and at least one year of post-qualification highly relevant employment completed in the last five years;</li>
<li>Qualification(s) assessed at AQF Certificate IV without a highly relevant field of study and at least two years of post-qualification highly relevant employment completed in the last five years;</li>
<li>Qualification(s) assessed at AQF Certificate III with a highly relevant field of study and at least three years of post-qualification highly relevant employment completed in the last five years;</li>
<p>According to Vetassess, if you have pre-qualification employment and if all of your employment is before your qualifications assessed at the AQF Certificate III or Certificate IV level, three years of highly relevant employment in addition to at least one year of highly relevant employment at an appropriate skill level completed in the last five years.</p>
