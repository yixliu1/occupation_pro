<h1>Occupation Name: Tennis Coach</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 452316</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: VETASSESS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Tennis Coach coaches, trains and instructs participants in tennis by analysing their performances and developing their abilities. Registration or licensing is required.</p>
<p>Tennis Coach may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>coaching, training and instructing sportspersons by analysing performances and developing abilities</li>
<li>planning and directing game strategies, developing play patterns and analysing game progress</li>
<li>motivating Sportspersons and supervising practice sessions</li>
<li>recruiting players and other coaching staff</li>
<li>arranging entries into sporting competitions</li>
<li>promoting sports and skills development, and overseeing the participation of young people in sport</li>
<li>officiating at sporting events to enforce rules</li>
<li>coordinating and directing sporting activities, and liaising with other officials to interpret and enforce rules and regulations relating to sport</li>
