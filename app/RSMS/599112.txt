<h1>Occupation Name: Legal Executive</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 599112</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: VETASSESS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Legal Executive acts for and on behalf of clients in the areas of property transfer, company and business law, trusts, wills, probate and litigation under the general supervision of a Barrister or Solicitor. Registration or licensing may be required.</p>
<p>Legal Executive may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>preparing, examining and advising on contracts of sale for properties and businesses</li>
<li>carrying out title searches and contacting government authorities to find out if any planned development, illegal building work and disputes could affect properties and businesses</li>
<li>preparing, examining and advising on mortgage documentation</li>
<li>negotiating the terms and conditions of, and exchanging, contracts of sale, paying deposits, arranging payment of stamp duty, checking for outstanding arrears and land tax obligations, and calculating adjustments for council and water rates</li>
<li>interviewing clients to determine the nature of issues, and receiving written information concerning cases from Legal Professionals and clients</li>
<li>conferring with clients and potential witnesses and drawing up statements and proposed affidavits in preparation for court proceedings</li>
<li>maintaining legal files</li>
<li>preparing, analysing and interpreting a variety of legal documents</li>
<li>assisting Legal Professionals to prepare cases for court by conducting investigations, undertaking research, arranging witness preparation and attendance, and preparing and filing court documents</li>
<li>may supervise Law Clerks and Legal Secretaries and take responsibility for their work</li>
<h2>Eligible applying visa subclasses</h2>
<p>This occupation is on the Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme Occupation List (RSMS ROL) , you may be eligible to apply for the following visa subclass:</p>
<li>Regional Sponsor Migration Scheme (subclass 187)</li>
<li>Skilled Employer Sponsored Regional (Provisional) visa (Subclass 494)</li>
<h2>Requirements for Skills Assessment</h2>
<p>Legal Executive is classified as Group C occupations by Vetassess. The criteria for skills assessment of Group C occupations are the following :</p>
<li>Qualification(s) assessed at least AQF Diploma level with a highly relevant field of study and at least one year of post qualification highly relevant employment in the last five years; </li>
<li>Qualification(s) assessed at least AQF Diploma level with an additional qualification at least AQF Certificate IV in a highly relevant field of study and at least one year of post-qualification highly relevant employment in the last five years; </li>
<li>Qualification(s) assessed at least AQF Diploma level without a highly relevant field of study and at least two years of post-qualification highly relevant employment in the last five years;</li>
<p>According to Vetassess, if you have pre-qualification employment and if all of your employment is before your qualification assessed at the AQF diploma level, three years of highly relevant employment in addition to at least one year of highly relevant employment completed in the last five years.</p>
