<h1>Occupation Name: Insurance Loss Adjuster</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 599612</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: VETASSESS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Insurance Loss Adjuster inspects and assesses the damage and loss to insured property and business, estimates insurance costs, and acts to minimise the cost of claims to an insurance company.</p>
<p>Insurance Loss Adjuster may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>examining scenes of incidents resulting in insurance claims to determine causes and effects</li>
<li>interviewing witnesses and claimants to obtain details required to assess the validity of claims and identify the parties responsible for accidents, damage and loss, and preparing statements and reports</li>
<li>inspecting damaged buildings, equipment and motor vehicles and estimating the cost of repairs</li>
<li>estimating business losses resulting from fire, theft and other business disruptions</li>
<li>reporting the extent of damage and estimated costs to the insurer</li>
<li>inspecting property, buildings and operations of commercial and industrial establishments to assess physical conditions and work practices</li>
<li>evaluating the adequacy of security, fire and related systems</li>
<li>preparing reports and recommending action to reduce risks</li>
<li>compiling data which influence the determination of premium rates</li>
