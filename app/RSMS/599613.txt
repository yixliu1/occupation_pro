<h1>Occupation Name: Insurance Risk Surveyor</h1>
<h1>ANZSCO Code: 599613</h1>
<h1>Assessment Authorities: VETASSESS</h1>
<h2>Job description </h2>
<p>Insurance Risk Surveyor inspects items and properties to evaluate conditions affecting underwriting standards, and develops and promotes safety programs.</p>
<p>Insurance Risk Surveyor may perform the following tasks:</p>
<li>examining scenes of incidents resulting in insurance claims to determine causes and effects</li>
<li>interviewing witnesses and claimants to obtain details required to assess the validity of claims and identify the parties responsible for accidents, damage and loss, and preparing statements and reports</li>
<li>inspecting damaged buildings, equipment and motor vehicles and estimating the cost of repairs</li>
<li>estimating business losses resulting from fire, theft and other business disruptions</li>
<li>reporting the extent of damage and estimated costs to the insurer</li>
<li>inspecting property, buildings and operations of commercial and industrial establishments to assess physical conditions and work practices</li>
<li>evaluating the adequacy of security, fire and related systems</li>
<li>preparing reports and recommending action to reduce risks</li>
<li>compiling data which influence the determination of premium rates</li>

<h2>Requirements for Skills Assessment</h2>
<p>Insurance Risk Surveyor is classified as Group C occupations by Vetassess. The criteria for skills assessment of Group C occupations are the following :</p>
<li>Qualification(s) assessed at least AQF Diploma level with a highly relevant field of study and at least one year of post qualification highly relevant employment in the last five years; </li>
<li>Qualification(s) assessed at least AQF Diploma level with an additional qualification at least AQF Certificate IV in a highly relevant field of study and at least one year of post-qualification highly relevant employment in the last five years; </li>
<li>Qualification(s) assessed at least AQF Diploma level without a highly relevant field of study and at least two years of post-qualification highly relevant employment in the last five years;</li>
<p>According to Vetassess, if you have pre-qualification employment and if all of your employment is before your qualification assessed at the AQF diploma level), three years of highly relevant employment in addition to at least one year of highly relevant employment completed in the last five years.</p>
